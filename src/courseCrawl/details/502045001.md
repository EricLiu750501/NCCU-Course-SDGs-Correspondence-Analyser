##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course is designed to enhance the Arabic language proficiency of third-year Arabic majors by focusing on reading and speaking skills during class sessions, and developing writing and listening skills through at-home assignments.

##  評量工具與策略、評分標準成效Evaluation Criteria
Quiz: 20%
Assignment: 30%
Mid-term: 20%
Fimal exam: 30%
  1. Assignments must follow the instructor's provided hints. Submissions that disregard these guidelines and contain AI-generated content will not be graded.
  2. Students accumulating three absences will not pass the course and will be ineligible to take the midterm and final examinations.
  3. Assignments must be submitted on time. Students caught utilizing electrical devices during quizzes or exams, plagiarizing in assignments or cheating in examinations will receive a failing grade.
  4. Students failing to complete the worksheets provided by the instructor will not receive a grade.
  5. Students requesting leave and applying for a make-up quiz will have their grade multiplied by eighty percent (80%). The make-up quiz must be completed within two weeks.
  6. Previewing the pre-recorded instructional audio and looking up unfamiliar vocabulary before class are required.
