##  課程簡介Course Description
Social media has become an integral part of modern society, influencing how we communicate, connect, and conduct business. This course delves into the complexities of managing and governing social media platforms effectively. Students will explore strategies for building and maintaining a strong social media presence, navigating ethical considerations, and mitigating risks associated with online engagement. Through case studies, discussions, and hands-on exercises, students will develop the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in the dynamic world of social media management.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course, developed in collaboration with Meta, aims to provide students with a deep understanding of Computer-Mediated Communication (CMC). Throughout the course, students will explore the multifaceted dimensions of CMC, covering both theoretical concepts and practical applications. They will delve into various theories and models related to CMC, gaining insights into how communication processes are shaped by digital technologies. Additionally, students will benefit from practical experiences drawn from industry professionals, learning about real-world applications of CMC through case studies and guest lectures. By integrating theoretical understanding with hands-on experience, this course offers students a comprehensive knowledge base, empowering them to become adept practitioners in the realm of digital communication.

##  評量工具與策略、評分標準成效Evaluation Criteria
**Course requirements**
  1. _Attendance:**10%**_  
Attendance is a fundamental requirement for this class, and instructors take it seriously. Your presence is expected at every session. From the second absence onwards, your final grade will be reduced by 2 points for each absence. However, those who have perfect attendance for the entire semester will receive an additional 2 points.  

  2. _Class Participation:**20%**_  
Active class participation is vital in this course, which blends aspects of both a lecture and a seminar. Your contributions are highly valued as they often stimulate deeper discussions on various topics. Please come prepared by completing the assigned readings before each class. Your insights and opinions are crucial for enriching our discussions.  

  3. _Mid-term Examination:**30%**_  
The midterm exam will encompass topics discussed in the first half of the semester and will consist of essay questions. It is recommended that you support your opinions with evidence.


  1. _Final Project Presentation and Writing:**40%**_  
The final project, which is a group project, offers flexibility in its format. You can opt for a case study, where you analyze how a particular platform handles user privacy, for example. Alternatively, you may choose to conduct a literature review paper, exploring the effects of digital media use on human attitudes or behaviors. Another option is to create a policy paper, making recommendations on regulatory or policy issues related to digital media.
