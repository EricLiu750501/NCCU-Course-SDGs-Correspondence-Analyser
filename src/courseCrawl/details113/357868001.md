##  課程簡介Course Description
This is a rigorous and demanding course and students are expected to devote their time in working their research topics.
This master course is aimed to give students an overview of empirical contributions of modern finance with a focus on corporate finance. The course will prepare students to conduct independent empirical finance research which could potentially leads to their dissertation. 
Note that this is a heavy-loading course designed for students who are interested in doing empirical finance research.
