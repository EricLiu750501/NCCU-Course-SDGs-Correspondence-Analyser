##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is designed for upper-level undergraduate students and graduate students, focusing on the field of gender, work, and care. It explores the evolving relationship between production and reproduction over time, as well as the processes of inclusion and exclusion in the labor market. The course addresses topics such as the historical and multifaceted dimensions of welfare politics, activation policies, migration, and care work, examining how gender and other structural categories intersect with production and reproduction practices. Key questions include: What constitutes work? How do wage labor norms shape concepts of equality and welfare? How is care (both paid and unpaid) defined? Why do governments implement family policies or work-family balance programs? What are global care chains, and how have they emerged? Why and how are inequalities sustained and reinforced within families, work organizations, markets, and state policies? How can these inequalities and challenges be addressed? The course critically examines work-family balance policies in Europe, the United States, and Asia (East Asia) to propose strategies for Taiwan’s future integration of labor market policies, human capital policies, and gender equality policies into a comprehensive "work-family balance policy." 本課程是針對大學部高年級生及碩士班學生所設計的課程，主要介紹性別、工作與照顧的領域，人們隨著時間的推移對生產(production)和再生產(reproduction)之間的關係及勞動力市場的納入(inclusion)和排除(exclusion)過程進行探索。本課程涉及福利政治的歷史和多元交織的面向、積極活化政策(activation policies)、移民和照顧工作等議題，探討性別和其他結構類別如何與生產和再生產實務相連結。什麼是工作？薪資工作規範如何支配平等和福利的理念？如何定義照顧(有酬與無酬的照顧)?政府為何要透過家庭政策或家庭工作平衡方案?什麼是全球照顧鏈？它們是如何出現的？不平等條件又為何在家庭、工作組織或市場和國家政策中得以維持和強化？如何因應這些不平等與挑戰？如何思辨工作與家庭平衡政策的歐洲政策和美國政策，以及亞洲(東亞)政策，以創造台灣未來融入勞動市場政策、人力資本政策和性別平等政策之「平衡工作與家庭政策」。

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
1. Understand and explain how production and reproduction, paid and unpaid work are interconnected, as well as their relationships with gender, age, class, race/ethnicity, and sexual orientation. Explore the balance between work and family policies from cultural and social policy perspectives, including "social justice theory" and the "gender equity approach" to work and family policies.
2. Understand the evolving organization of work in the global economy, as well as family work migration related to family and caregiving work, and the development of global care chains. Independently articulate and connect key perspectives, boundaries, concepts, and themes regarding gender, work, and migration. Critically and independently reflect on power relations within and beyond the labor market.
3.The course emphasizes active learning, requiring students to apply knowledge in practice and critically reflect on it. Through various teaching strategies, such as lectures, group discussions, and individual and group exercises on selected topics, students are expected to actively participate in all seminars. They should conduct independent analyses of the relationship between production and reproduction in both oral and written forms (word file), complete thematic presentations in PPT. 表單的底部
1、理解並解釋生產和再生產、有酬和無酬工作如何相互關聯，以及與性別、年齡、階級、種族/民族和性別取向的關係；從文化與社會政策觀點探討平衡工作與家庭政策: 包括「社會正義理論(social justice theory)」與「性別公平觀點(gender equity approach)」的工作與家庭政策; 
2、了解全球經濟中不斷變化的工作組織，以及了解與家庭和照護工作相關的家庭工作移民，以及全球照護鏈的發展。獨立闡述並關聯有關性別、工作和移民的中心觀點、分界線、概念和主題。獨立且批判性地反思勞動市場內外的權力關係。
3、課程著重修課學生的主動學習，即將知識付諸實踐並批判性地反思知識。透過不同的教學策略，例如講座、小組討論以及針對選定主題的各種形式的個人和小組練習。學生應積極參加所有專題討論，並以口頭和書面形式對生產和再生產之間的關係進行獨立分析，完期專題PPT發表及期末專題報告(word檔)以通過課程。
