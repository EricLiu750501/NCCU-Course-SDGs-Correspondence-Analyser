##  課程簡介Course Description
This course aims to equip you with the basic knowledge of research design, methods, and skills. It will help you to become familiar with the process of developing a research project. To help you begin to master research methods and appreciate interdisciplinary research methods, the course will introduce you to a range of qualitative, quantitative, and integrative research methods. We will explore research projects, academic conventions, and skills across various disciplines through readings, lectures, workshops, and sharing practitioners' experiences. The skills that you will learn in this course will not only be useful to you in your academic studies but also in your future professional life.
這門課目的在於協助你掌握研究設計、方法和技能的基本知識，引導你完成發展一份研究計畫(報告)的過程。本課程將介紹一系列量化、質化與整合性研究方法，幫助妳開始掌握研究方法，並學習欣賞不同學科、跨領域的研究取徑、特長與侷限。本課程將介紹一系列量化、質化與整合性研究方法。我們將藉由閱讀、講授、工作坊、研究者的個人經驗分享，一同探索在永續科學中，不同學科領領域的研究計畫、學術規範與寫作技巧。本課程分享的研究方法與技巧將為你的學業表現與未來工作如虎添翼。

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon full completion of this course, students will be able to
  * providing basic knowledge and tools for social scientific research
  * understanding a range of qualitative and quantitative research methods
  * understanding different genres of academic research design and writing in social sciences
  * strengthen students’ ability to develop academic research projects and enhance capacities of academic communications
- 提供社會科學研究的基本知識和工具
- 瞭解一系列量化、質化與整合性研究方法
- 瞭解社會科學中不同類型的學術研究設計和寫作
- 加強學生發展研究計畫與學術溝通的能力
