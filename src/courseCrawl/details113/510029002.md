##  課程簡介Course Description
Development of literacy in Thai for those trained in basic reading and writing skills. Focus on Thai script reading, reading comprehension, script writing, and composition. Thai vocabulary development and reading skill improvement in non-fictional texts.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students have upper elementary skills in systematic reading and writing of Thai scripts. With focus in reading practice from Thai passages to improve vocabulary skills, context clues, and analogies, the class plans that students develop personal reading habits to explore different literary genres as independent readers.
