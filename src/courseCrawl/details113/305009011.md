##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is intended to introduce the basics of human resource management (HRM). We will mainly focus on the strategic meaning of HRM functions and practices (e.g., staffing, training and development, performance management, compensation management).

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The goal of this introductory course is to equip students with the basic understanding of HRM functions and practices and their strategic meanings. Students are expected to
  * understand the strategic meaning of basic HRM functions and practices
  * learn how the institutional contexts shape HRM practices
  * engage in in-class case and understand business cases via HRM lenses
