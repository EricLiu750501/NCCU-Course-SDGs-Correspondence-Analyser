##  課程簡介Course Description
The objective of this course is to provide the students with the essential and fundamental concepts of elementary statistics. The topics include descriptive statistical analysis, probability and distributions, random sampling and sampling distribution, statistical inference.
本課程不開放加簽。

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
At this course, the students should be able to 
1. acquire a comprehensive grasp of fundamental Statistics;
2 define a real problem in statistical jargon; 
3. conduct and present a descriptive summary of a real dataset.
Moreover, students need to be familiar with at least one statistical software such as EXCEL, Minitab, or R.
