##  課程簡介Course Description
Data science has been popular in recent years, and statistics is a fundamental and important subject to support data science. This course aims to introduce undergraduate students the basic concepts in statistics. In this course, students will learn different techniques to analyze data, including data visualization, probability theory, statistical inference, and model construction. After this course, students are expected to have knowledge of statistics and have abilities to analyze data.
