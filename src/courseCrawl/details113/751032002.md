##  課程簡介Course Description
This course covers the topic on martingale theory. It contains three main theorems of martingale theory, i.e, convergence theorems, martingale inequalities and optional sampling. We will also discuss some applications of martingale theory. These will include option pricing in financial mathematics, Kalman-Bucy filtering and other results in various mathematics branches.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Once students have acquired the ability to manipulate martingales, they can almost go into any subfields in probability.The classical results in probability including law of large numbers , central limit theorem , Markov processes and other related subjects in mathematical physics are all within reach.
