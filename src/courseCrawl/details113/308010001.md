##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程目的在於介紹 (1) 財產保險保單，包括保單類型與契約條款，以及(2) 產險公司之經營。
Purpose of this course: This course is to introduce (1) the property insurance policies, including product types and contractual provisions, and (2) the operations of nonlife insurance company.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程在於說明財產保險之基本原理，課程內容以分析我國保單為重心，但同時會與美國保單略做比較。此外，亦探討產險公司之經營，以及產險市場環境。學生修課之後將對於財產保險之保單與產險公司經營有所瞭解。
This course introduces the basic principles of property insurance, which emphasizes on the insurance policies in Taiwan but also briefly discusses the US. insurance policies. In addition, the course presents the operations of nonlife insurance company and the environmental factors of insurance market. Studetns can learn the contractual provisions of nonlife insurance policies and the operations of nonlife insurance company from this course.
