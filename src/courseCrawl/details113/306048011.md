##  課程簡介Course Description
This is a required course for undergraduate students in Management Information Systems. In recent years, the notion of analytics and artificial intelligence (AI) has been shaking many industries. A Lot of development in analytics and AI is grounded on Management Science models and solution approaches. Since its inception, Management Science has had tremendous influences on how firms make strategic, tactic, and operational decisions for different functions including marketing, production, R&D, human resources, finance, etc. The primary goal of this course is to sharpen students’ quantitative modeling skills.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
After finishing this course, students are expected to have a solid grasp of important optimization, simulation, and prediction techniques, in addition to having a good understanding about potential applications of learnt techniques. To set the expectation right, this is going to be a time-consuming course. Nonetheless, we assure you this will be a rewarding experience, as long as you are willing to learn in- and off-class. We aim to enable you to apply modeling techniques to solve problems.
