##  課程簡介Course Description
This course focuses on students’ conversational skills in pre-advanced Arabic, aiming to improve students’ pronunciation, listening and speaking abilities, and familiarity with colloquial modern standard Arabic.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students will learn and practise basic Arabic conversation revolving various topics. By the end of the semester, students should be able to engage in a complex conversation or debate with native speakers.
