##  課程簡介Course Description
The goal of this semester is to develop competence in using English for presenting information in both informal and formal settings, with a particular focus on speaking and reading. In terms of reading, the objective is to comprehend articles on a variety of subjects, enabling students to acquire specific knowledge that can be applied to speaking tasks, such as constructing different types of speeches or participating in discussions.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students will learn to communicate confidently in English in both informal and formal settings. They are expected to recognize, respond to, and converse using the appropriate register when interacting in social contexts that require formal communication. In situations where English is necessary to successfully complete a task, students will be able to present their decisions upon task completion. Finally, students should feel confident speaking in front of an audience. They should be able to give clear directions, express opinions, and deliver impromptu speeches to the entire class.
