##  課程簡介Course Description
Writing, like any other skilled profession, is a craft. Mastering such a craft; however, takes time—and so does refining your writing skills. This course is designed for those who are patient, detail-oriented, and eager to practice and polish up their writing. We will begin with the fundamentals—brainstorming topics, developing ideas, structuring sentences, and organizing arguments—and progress to more subtle elements, such as word choice, rhetorical devices, and seamless paragraph transitions. Through in-class writing exercises, peer reviews, and writing assignments, students will sharpen the skills needed to write clearly and effectively within their chosen topic.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By the end of the course, students will:
  1. Develop a sense of appreciation for the craftsmanship of writing, including brainstorming, outlining, summarizing, supporting arguments, and concluding.
  2. Communicate ideas clearly and effectively in written form
  3. Expand and improve their English vocabulary 
  4. Master the skills of extracting and synthesizing contents from written works and audiovisual materials.
