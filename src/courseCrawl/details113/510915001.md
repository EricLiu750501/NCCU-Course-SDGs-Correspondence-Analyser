##  課程簡介Course Description
Concepts and Diversity in Southeast Asian Performing Art Cultures, Socio-cultural Settings as having effects in production and practice of performing arts. Fundamental skills in performing arts of Indonesia, Thailand, and Vietnam. (In the 113/2 semester, the class will apply intensive method and classes are on May 6,10,11,13,17,18,20 (All Tues, Sat, and Sun) . There are 4 sessions on Sat and Sun and are from 2 pm to 5 pm. In this semester all class activities will finish by May 27, 2025. Please refer to the updated class schedule.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
To enhance knowledge in basic concepts, cultural diversity, and theoretical thinking and skill of traditional performing arts in Southeast Asian socio-cultural settings with foci on Indonesian, Thai, and Vietnamese Performing Arts Culture.
