##  課程簡介Course Description
Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS), and Remote Sensing (RS) are three essential components supporting Geomatics research. With the great capability to observe the global ground surface based on spaceborne sensors, remote sensing has been increasingly applied in handling real-world environmental issues nowadays. In order to promote the understanding of remote sensing and further multi-disciplinary research in the Social Science College, the introduction of RS and its applications, in particular the connection with SDGs, will be covered in this course.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The course mainly covers basic concepts of remote sensing, image processing and interpretation, multispectral, thermal infrared and hyperspectral sensing, microwave and LiDAR sensing, etc. The following goals are expected to be achieved:
1. Understand the basic principles of remote sensing;  
2. Learn remote sensing data processing operations;  
3. Have the capability to use remote sensing in practical applications, including SDGs investigations;  
4. Develop the connection ability to learn independently
