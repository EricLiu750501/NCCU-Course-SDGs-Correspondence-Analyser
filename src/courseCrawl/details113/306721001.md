##  課程簡介Course Description
IS (Information Systems) project has been a critical management target, and it covers various research topics and problems. In viewing that many managers and scholars are concerned with the issues of mitigating IS project’s uncertainties, this course will be focused on introducing to our students the application of specific knowledge in the related literature and pragmatic techniques or solutions to clarify and address such issues.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course aims to bring to our students a suitable set of learning objectives and outcomes  
including but not limited to what follows: 1) learning IS project strategic goal, estimation, prioritization, resource allocation, and portfolio management or so forth., 2) learning IS project risk identification and mitigation, development monitoring or evaluation, problem-solving, and so forth., 3) learning IS project team communication, leadership, stakeholder, governance, collaboration, integration, procurement, sourcing, and so forth, and 4) learning the real-world solutions, practical approaches, and so forth.
