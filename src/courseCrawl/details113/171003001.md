##  課程簡介Course Description
課程簡介: 本課程主要在探討學校行政相關理論與政策脈絡，將以實務運作之議題為主。其中包括校長領導、成員溝通、組織理論、特色課程、知識管理、與班級經營等課題，俾利學生將來能運用於教育職場。
Course description : The main objective of this graduate course is to provide students with an understanding of the practical application of theoretical and policy contexts of school administration. Students in this course will also explore topics such as principal leadership, member communication, organizational theory, specialized school programs, knowledge management, and classroom management; giving them practical skills for working in an educational setting.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
學習目標與成效:
  * 使學生了解學校行政的理論內涵與運作現況並選取適合的議題進行討論與研究。
  * 瞭解學校行政組織之特性、研究典範。
  * 瞭解學校行政組織之相關理論（動機、領導、溝通、決策等）。
  * 瞭解學校行政組織之運作與個案研究。
  * 瞭解學校政策之擬定並進行討論。
  * 瞭解學校行政組織之爭議議題並進行討論。
Course objectives : Specific course objectives are as follows: 1) To gain an understanding of the theoretical and operational aspects of school administration, and select topics for discussion and research; 2) To understand the characteristics of school administrative organizations and research paradigms; 3) To understand theories related to school administration (motivation, leadership, communication, decision making, etc.); 4) To understand the operation of school administrative organizations through case studies; 5) To understand and discuss the development of school policies; and 6) To understand and discuss controversial issues in school administration.
