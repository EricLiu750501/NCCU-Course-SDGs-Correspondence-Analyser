##  課程簡介Course Description
This course focuses on the specialized vocabulary, communication skills, and cultural insights essential for professionals in the Meetings, Incentives, Conferencing, and Exhibitions (MICE) industry. Tailored for students aspiring to excel in global event management, the curriculum covers real-world scenarios, negotiations, planning, and execution of international events.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course serves as an introduction to the MICE industry, encompassing Meetings, Incentive Travel, Conferences, and Exhibitions. It offers learners a comprehensive platform for refining their language skills, specifically through various speaking tasks related to the hospitality sector. The course is meticulously crafted to refine learners’ communication abilities and boost their confidence when engaging with others in MICE-related scenarios. Learners will receive support and problem-solving assistance as they practice.
The objectives of the course are as follows:
* To acquaint learners with the fundamental principles and practices of the MICE industry.
* To hone learners’ communication skills, particularly in the context of MICE-related language usage.
* To foster the ability of learners to deliver impromptu speeches effectively in diverse MICE-related settings.
