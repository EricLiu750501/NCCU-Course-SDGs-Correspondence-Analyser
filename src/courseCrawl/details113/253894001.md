##  課程簡介Course Description
在全球近190多個國家中，發展中國家的數量是最多的。但是，在現實主義與權力政治的國際體系結構中，以及全球化的趨勢下，發展中國家的自主國家發展的能力與國際影響力卻呈現出受到大國高度影響與制約的現象。本課程規劃從宏觀與微觀的兩個面向，就國際體系結構、國內政經發展這兩個面向切入觀察如下議題：(1)發展中國家的政治發展與外交政策取向；G20及聯合國等國際組織中的發展中國家；(3)就亞洲，拉丁美洲及非洲的主要發展中大國進行國別探討。
There are over 190 countries in the world now and the amount of developing countries is largest. 
However, because of comparative limited begiven capability in the world, the development capabilities and international influence of developing countries are highly influenced and restricted by big powers.
This course is aim to analyze the following topics from both macro and micro perspectives, including the structure of the international system and domestic political and economic development: (1) Political development and foreign policy orientation of developing countries; international organizations such as the G20 and the United Nations developing countries in Asia; (3) conduct country-specific discussions on major developing countries in Asia, Latin America and Africa.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程以逐宏觀與微觀的兩種形式，步培養與深化學生對於發展中國家的國家發展與外交政策的理解，以期讓未來可能從事涉外的學生在未來規劃前往海外發展時，能夠節省摸索的時間。
The main purpose of this course aims to cultivate and deepen students' understanding of the national development and foreign policies of developing countries, using macro and micro perspective. 
This course requires students to give an oral report (15 minutes) in class first, and then submit a final report (6,000~12,000 words) at the end of the semester.
The teacher will provide a list of suggested reading articles both in in the class Chinese and foreign languages (available in the second week) and ask students to select at least one paper every other week in advance in order to evaluate students' independent reading ability.
