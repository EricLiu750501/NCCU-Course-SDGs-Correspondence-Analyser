##  課程簡介Course Description
This course provides an overview of virtual asset and real-world asset tokenization, focusing on blockchain technologies, market trends, and regulatory challenges. Students will explore tokenization applications in industries like real estate and finance, examine tokenomics, and address compliance issues. Through lectures, case studies, and projects, the course prepares students to analyze and apply tokenization concepts in emerging fields like DeFi and NFTs.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. Understand the fundamental concepts and technical framework of virtual asset and real-world asset tokenization.
  2. Analyze current market trends, regulatory environments, and legal challenges.
  3. Implement simple tokenization use cases in different industry scenarios.
  4. Develop critical thinking and interdisciplinary knowledge to address challenges in this emerging field.
