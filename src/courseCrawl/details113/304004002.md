##  課程簡介Course Description
The objective of this course is to provide the students with the essential and fundamental concepts of elementary linear algebra. At the end of this course, the students should be able to apply the tool of linear algebra to other topics, especially those statistics related. (除了本系學生，不開放人工加簽)

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The topics include linear equations and matrices, determinants, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, diagonalization, orthogonality, general vector spaces and statistical applications.
