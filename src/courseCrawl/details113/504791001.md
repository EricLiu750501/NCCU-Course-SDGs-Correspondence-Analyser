##  課程簡介Course Description
Music is an international language intelligible regardless of geographical, cultural, religious or political borders. During the course Czech Artificial Music, lessons will be sequenced from teacher´s presentations and the following activities such as active listening, reading and discussion of the compositions, literature and important events connected or associated with each of them. Last but not least the lessons will include basic vocabulary on the topic and practicing pronunciation by actively getting acquainted with the lyrics of popular and folk songs.
Content of this lecture provides following areas:
  * Basic information about Czech musical history and composers
  * Getting acquaintance with Czech famous musical compositions and its forms
  * Basic information about Czech musical life (festivals, other events) and institutions

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course is designed for students with some knowledge of Czech (from Czech language study program) as well as for students with limited or almost no knowledge of the Czech language, who are interested in Czech Republic and its culture.
For all students this course should offer an overview and basic information about rich Czech musical tradition.
The course should provide students of the Czech program with new interesting stimuli for the practical study of Czech, mainly improving their listening skills and pronunciation. This should lead to greater confidence in active skills (speaking and writing).
