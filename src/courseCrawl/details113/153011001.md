##  課程簡介Course Description
The goal of this course will be the development of the skills needed for graduate students to function and thrive in the English-language academic world. These skills will include writing, reading, speaking, and listening in different academic contexts. Students will gain a familiarity with the ways academic research and discussions are typically presented in English-language monographs, journal articles, book reviews, academic job interviews, classrooms, and conferences.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The ultimate aim of the course is to make graduate students more effective at engaging with English-language scholars and scholarship. Increasing their familiarity with English-language academia will enhance the value of their own graduate research by broadening the scope of the debates they will be able to participate in and the past scholarship they will be able to access. Enhancing students’ ability to engage in English will also offer them a greater range of international opportunities to present their work and seek employment after graduation.
