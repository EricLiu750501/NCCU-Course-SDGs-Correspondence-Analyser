##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程延續初級越語課程I、進一步發掘聽、說、讀、寫的初級技巧與更深入的越南文化。本課程著重於現今越南日常生活的各個面向、提供學生穩固的越南語基礎。
The course is a continuation of elementary Vietnamese I, which is further to develop the integrated skills of listening, speaking, reading and writing at elementary level, together with deeper understanding of Vietnam culture. The lessons have the focus on various aspects of life of present-day Vietnam, in order to provide students with solid foundation in using Vietnamese.
