##  課程簡介Course Description
本閱讀課程的主要目標在於深入教授學生關於泰文閱讀的基礎要素，特別是詞彙這閱讀 的關鍵元素。詞彙在閱讀過程中扮演著至關重要的角色，事實上，它們被視為閱讀的基本構 成單位之一，對閱讀理解和語言理解具有決定性影響。The primary objective of this reading course is to provide students with an in-depth understanding of the fundamental elements of Thai language reading, with a special focus on vocabulary as a critical component of reading. Vocabulary plays an indispensable role in the reading process and is indeed regarded as one of the fundamental building blocks of reading comprehension and language understanding.
在閱讀的過程中，首要的步驟即是理解文本中的詞彙。讀者必須能夠辨識並理解每個詞 彙的內容，包括單字的意義、拼字、以及發音。然而，值得一提的是，不同的語言擁有各自 獨特的書寫系統、詞法結構，以及構詞法規則。Throughout the process of reading, the foremost step is the comprehension of vocabulary within the text. Readers must be able to identify and understand the content of each vocabulary item, including its meaning, spelling, and pronunciation. However, it is worth noting that different languages possess their unique writing systems, lexical structures, and morphology rules.
在本課程中，我們將引導學生深入瞭解泰文詞彙的重要性，並特別聚焦在泰文八種尾輔 音的熟悉。透過深入探討泰國詞法和構詞法，以及進行泰文基本句型的實際練習，學生將建 立扎實的泰文詞彙基礎。這將不僅提高學生的泰文閱讀能力，還將加強他們的語言技能，使 他們能夠更自如地應用泰文於不同情境中。In this course, we will guide students in gaining a profound appreciation for the significance of Thai vocabulary, with a particular emphasis on familiarizing them with the eight final consonants in the Thai language. Through an in-depth exploration of Thai lexical and morphological aspects, coupled with practical exercises involving basic Thai sentence structures, students will establish a strong foundation in Thai vocabulary. This will not only enhance their proficiency in Thai reading but also bolster their language skills, enabling them to apply Thai effectively in various contexts.
總之，這閱讀課程將為學生提供一個全面的泰文詞彙學習體驗，有助於為未來更深入的 泰文閱讀和語言學習奠定堅實的基礎。In summary, this reading course will provide students with a comprehensive learning experience in Thai vocabulary, paving the way for more advanced Thai reading and language learning endeavors in the future.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
1.了解詞彙的重要性 (Understand the Significance of Vocabulary)  
了解詞彙在閱讀過程中扮演的關鍵角色。Gain an appreciation for the pivotal role that vocabulary plays in the reading process.
2.詞彙識別與理解 (Vocabulary Identification and Comprehension)  
培養識別和理解單詞內容的能力，包括其意義、拼寫和發音。Develop the ability to identify and comprehend the content of individual vocabulary items, including their meanings, spellings, and pronunciations.
3.理解語言變體 (Understanding Language Variation)  
認識不同語言中書寫系統、詞彙結構和形態規則的多樣性。Recognize the diversity of writing systems, lexical structures, and morphological rules in different languages.
4.泰文詞彙要素 (Focus on Thai Vocabulary Elements)  
熟悉泰文八種尾輔音，強調它們在泰文詞彙中的重要性。Familiarize oneself with the eight final consonants in the Thai language, emphasizing their importance in Thai vocabulary.
5.泰文詞彙應用 (Application of Thai Vocabulary)  
在實際練習中應用所獲得的泰文詞彙、泰國詞法以及基本句型知識。Apply the acquired knowledge of Thai vocabulary, Thai morphology, and basic sentence structures in practical exercises.
6.提升語言技能 (Enhanced Language Skills)  
提高總體泰文語言能力，特別關注閱讀理解和實用語言運用。Improve overall Thai language proficiency, with a particular focus on reading comprehension and practical language use.
總之，本閱讀課程的目標是為學生提供全面的泰文詞彙學習體驗，強調詞彙在閱讀和語言理 解中的關鍵作用。課程結束時，學生將建立堅實的泰文詞彙基礎，使他們能夠提升泰文閱讀 技能和語言能力。In summary, the goal of this reading course is to provide students with a comprehensive learning experience in Thai vocabulary, emphasizing the pivotal role of vocabulary in reading and language comprehension. By the end of the course, students will have established a strong foundation in Thai vocabulary, enabling them to enhance their Thai reading skills and language proficiency in various contexts.
