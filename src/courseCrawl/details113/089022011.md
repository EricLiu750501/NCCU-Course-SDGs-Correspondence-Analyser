##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程僅供校內外籍學位生修習。本課程協助由初級進入中級的華語學習者能夠正確地成段表達與理解的能力。課程內容以在台灣的日常生活及校園活動對話為主，讓中級學習者在學習語言知識同時，也能增強語境意識。
This course is only available to foreign degree students at NCCU. This course helps Chinese language learners from beginning to intermediate levels develop the ability to express and understand in paragraphs correctly. The course content is mainly based on conversations about daily life and campus activities in Taiwan, allowing intermediate learners to enhance contextual awareness while learning language knowledge.
