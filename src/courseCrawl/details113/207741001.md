##  課程簡介Course Description
課程簡介  
AI應用研究已成為21世紀的重要學科，其演算法已廣泛融入日常生活，例如近年快速崛起的生成式AI（Generative AI，簡稱GenAI），如ChatGPT。不僅提升了城市生活的便利性，AI科技更深刻地改變了我們生活方式與對城市規劃的思維。如何運用AI技術，設計出更智慧、便利、環境永續、安全且宜居的未來城市，是本課程的核心探討方向之一。
課程內容涵蓋AI演算法的基本原理與發展歷史，致力於建立學生的AI素養。此外，學生將學習運用各種AI技巧與工具，包括圖形、文字與語音處理。課堂著重於實作，學生將透過組隊合作、討論與實務操作，進行資料蒐集、AI神經網路訓練、成果驗證，並完成應用報告。此課程全面培養學生的理論基礎與實作能力，為未來AI應用奠定堅實基礎。
Course Description
AI application research has become a critical field of study in the 21st century, with its algorithms widely integrated into daily life. A prime example is the rapid rise of Generative AI (GenAI), such as ChatGPT. Beyond enhancing urban convenience, AI technology has profoundly transformed our lifestyles and perspectives on urban planning. One of the core focuses of this course is exploring how to leverage AI to design smarter, more convenient, sustainable, safe, and livable future cities.
The course covers the fundamental principles and historical development of AI algorithms, aiming to build students' foundational literacy in AI. Additionally, students will learn to apply various AI techniques and tools, including those for graphics, text, and voice processing. With a strong emphasis on hands-on practice, the course involves team collaboration, discussions, and practical activities, such as data collection, AI neural network training, result validation, and the completion of application reports. This comprehensive approach equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, laying a solid foundation for future applications of AI.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
課程目標與學習成效  
在本課程中，學生將學習城市永續生活設計的原則、AI演算法的演變歷史與基礎原理，以及如何應用現有的AI資源。學生將以團隊合作的方式規劃資源利用、進行城市規劃設計、執行AI專案，並透過書面及口頭報告展示成果，同時開發相關的AI應用程式。
  1. 通過學習各種AI應用軟體、研究企業ESG文件或實際土地規劃應用案例，獲得深度AI應用的專業能力。
  2. 以應用AI進行創新城市規劃設計並以英文進行報告，展現團隊合作與專題製作能力。
  3. 使用生成式AI（如ChatGPT）或預測式AI（如VisLab機器視覺軟體），學習並掌握結合人類智慧與AI的新型混合人機協同學習模式。
Course Objectives and Learning Outcomes  
In this course, students will learn the principles of sustainable urban living design, the evolution and foundational concepts of AI algorithms, and how to utilize existing AI resources. Through teamwork, students will plan resource allocation, undertake urban planning design, execute AI projects, and present their findings through written and oral reports while developing related AI applications.
  1. Acquire advanced AI application skills by learning various AI software tools, analyzing corporate ESG documents, or studying real-world land planning case applications.
  2. Apply AI in innovative urban planning designs and present results in English, demonstrating teamwork and project management capabilities.
  3. Explore and master new hybrid human-machine collaborative learning models that integrate human intelligence with AI, using tools such as generative AI (e.g., ChatGPT) and predictive AI (e.g., VisLab machine vision software).
