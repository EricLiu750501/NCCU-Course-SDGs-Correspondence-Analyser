Welcome to the Global Immersion Program in Asia – Taiwan Insights (Master Program) at National Chengchi University (NCCU), Taiwan. This intensive program is designed for members of PIM, AAPBS, IBSNet, and all partner schools, offering an enriching opportunity to explore Taiwanese business dynamics and cultural innovation. Through a multi-disciplinary curriculum guided by esteemed faculty and industry experts, participants will gain valuable insights into the economic landscape, innovation, and leadership practices that define Taiwan’s place in the global market.
The program blends academic coursework with practical applications, enabling participants to engage in various business fields such as leadership, entrepreneurship, innovation, intellectual property management, and international business strategies. Additionally, cultural experiences and company visits will allow participants to connect classroom knowledge with real-world applications in Taiwan’s business and cultural settings.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon completion of this program, students will:
  * Understand Taiwan’s Economic Landscape: Develop a comprehensive understanding of Taiwan’s economy, from its historical transformation to its current status as a global high-tech leader
  * Analyze the Impact of Technological Advancements: Assess how technological innovations and intellectual property management drive Taiwan’s competitive edge in global markets.
  * Explore Leadership and Team Dynamics: Gain insights into effective leadership strategies and team dynamics in modern organizations, particularly within Taiwan’s business culture, through experiential learning and case studies.
  * Evaluate Entrepreneurial and Innovation Strategies: Examine the role of creativity and innovation in new product design and entrepreneurship. Understand how Taiwanese companies foster innovation to maintain a competitive advantage in global markets.
  * Understand Global Business Practices: Learn about international tax challenges faced by multinational corporations operating in Taiwan and Asia. Develop strategic thinking to navigate the complex regulatory landscape affecting global businesses.
  * Build Professional Networks: Cultivate a global network by connecting with fellow participants, faculty, and industry professionals. These relationships will foster potential collaborations and career opportunities across diverse business sectors.
