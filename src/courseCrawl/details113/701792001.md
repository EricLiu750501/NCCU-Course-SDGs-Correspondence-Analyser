##  課程簡介Course Description
The purpose of this course is to develop and analyze the probability models that evolve over time and to provide the students with a fundamental understanding of probabilistic methods. Topics will include probability theory, Poisson processes, renewal processes, Markov chains, and Markov processes.
Prerequisites : Probability Thoery, Calculus and basic Linear Algebra

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students will understand and learn how to describe the theory of stochastic processes, especially for Markov processes, Poisson processes and renewal processes and conduct calculations with transition probabilities and related probabilities and moments to solve problems.
