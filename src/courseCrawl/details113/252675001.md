##  課程簡介Course Description
With the widespread application of Artificial Intelligence in political and social lives, how it will impact and change human political life, social relations, and democratic forms has begun to attract the attention of social scientists and humanities scholars. The first half of this course will focus on the core issues of political philosophy, and lead students to explore how this technological revolution affects important values ​​in human political life, such as power, freedom, justice, and equality. The second half of this course will focus on democracy, leading students to read relevant texts and to think and discuss the impact of AI on the basic principles and mechanisms of democracy, such as political judgment, political participation, and social trust.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course aims to guide students in reflecting on the relationship between AI and democratic politics. As AI is increasingly applied in the public domain, the course focuses on cultivating ethical thinking and moral judgment related to AI. The following are the teaching objectives:
  1. Understanding the Basics and Applications of AI
  2. Exploring the Relationship between AI and Democratic Politics
  3. Cultivating Ethical Thinking in AI
  4. Developing Moral Judgment Abilities
