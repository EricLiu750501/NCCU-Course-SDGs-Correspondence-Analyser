##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is concerned with the probabilistic analysis of algorithms. We will recall some tools from Discrete Mathematics and from Real and Complex Asymptotics. Then, we will consider algorithms and data structures and analyze their average case behavior as well as deeper stochastic properties. The course is suitable for students who are interested in applications of discrete mathematics.
