##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程之設計針對具越南語初級能力的學生，培養其中級聽力能力。此課程以學生爲中心，因此大部分的課堂時間會集中於討論與活動，並以課前的小組準備作爲基礎。 希望通過討論提高學生理解主題、以及培養批判性思考的能力。  
This course is designed for students who have already achieved an elementary competence in Vietnamese so as to develop their listening ability at intermediate levels. The course is in a student-centered format which means that most of the class time will be spent in discussion and activities based on group preparation before the class time. Students are expected to improve their ability to adequate listening ability to understand the subject matter and develop critical thinking skills through discussion.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
選修本課程，學生將建立以下基礎：
On the completion of this course, students will be able to:
詞彙 Vocabulary :
  * 學會運用在正式與非正式場合中的新詞彙。
Gain new vocabulary used in both formal and informal situations.
  * 辨認純越語、漢越詞、外來詞或混種詞等詞彙類型之不同處。
Distinguish types of vocabulary which are pure Vietnamese or Sino-Vietnamese or loanwords or mixed words.
聽力 Listening :
  * 能夠達到足以理解與專業領域相關的一般對話、演講、辯論、時事議題、廣播新聞等的聽力能力。
Be able to acquire listening proficiency sufficient to comprehend casual conversations, speech, debates, current issues, and broadcast news with regard to specialized fields.
口說 Speaking :
  * 學生們能夠在聽者面前更自在的發言、發表意見與同儕反饋，並能以正確的發音與自然的表達方式使用越南語報告。
Students are expected to feel more comfortable speaking in front of an audience, express opinions, peer feedback, and make presentations with accurate pronunciation and natural expressions in Vietnamese.
文化知識 Cultural knowledge _:_
  * 在不同情況下使用越南語以獲取文化知識。
Acquire cultural knowledge through the use of the Vietnamese language in different situations.
自主學習 Self-Learning :
  * 本課程鼓勵學生自主學習。 通過交流和小組活動，學生可以學習如何提高主動的聽力能力、自信地說話、對小組回饋做出建設性的貢獻、結合口語與非口語以適當地使用語言，以及積極地給予和接受同儕具建設性的反饋。
This course is designed to encourage autonomous learning. Through the interactions and group work activities, students are expected to learn how to sharpen their active listening skills, speak with confidence, contribute constructively to group feedback, integrate verbal and nonverbal language to support a message, and actively give and receive constructive feedback.
越南語檢定標準 Vietnamese language proficiency level:
  * 達到B1 – B2(中級標準)
Be able to reach B1 – B2 (Intermediate)
