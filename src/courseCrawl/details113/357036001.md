##  課程簡介Course Description
This is a seminar co-hosted by the departments of finance, banking, and international trade. Scholars will be invited to present their most concurrent research work. PhD students in the three departments are expected to join this seminar.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The objective is to increase the interaction across different areas of study in finance and economics, to build up the research connection across different research institutes, and to enrich the research contents across participants.
