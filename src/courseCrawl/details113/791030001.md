##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程建議先修科目線性代數、計算機概論，非強制；上課自備電腦。
隨著量子計算技術的快速發展，傳統基於數論問題（如RSA和ECC）的密碼系統將面臨無法抵禦量子計算機攻擊的挑戰。後量子密碼學即是能抵抗量子計算機攻擊的密碼技術，可確保未來數位通訊、資料安全，仍至身份驗證等安全隱私的技術。本課程將深入探討目前最具潛力的後量子密碼技術，包括格密碼學、碼密碼學、多變量密碼學、雜湊函數密碼學，也將簡介全同態加密等進階的密碼技術。
With the rapid development of quantum computing technology, traditional cryptographic systems based on number-theoretic problems (such as RSA and ECC) will face the challenge of being unable to withstand attacks from quantum computers. Post-quantum cryptography refers to cryptographic techniques that can resist quantum computing attacks, ensuring the security and privacy of future digital communications, data, and even identity verification. This course will introduce the most promising post-quantum cryptographic methods, including lattice-based cryptography, code-based cryptography, multivariate cryptography, and hash-based cryptography. It will also introduce advanced cryptographic concepts such as fully homomorphic encryption.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. 學習傳統密碼學與量子計算的基礎知識
  2. 瞭解 Shor 演算法對傳統密碼系統的威脅及量子計算的基本概念
  3. 學習後量子密碼學中的數學
  4. 學習後量子密碼技術與其應用
  5. 瞭解國際標準化組織 (如NIST) 的後量子密碼學標準。
  6. 學習進階密碼學技術 (如全同態加密)
  1. Learn the fundamentals of traditional cryptography
  2. Understand the threats posed by Shor's algorithm to traditional cryptographic systems and grasp basic concepts of quantum computing
  3. Study the mathematical foundations of post-quantum cryptography
  4. Learn post-quantum cryptographic techniques and their applications
  5. Understand the post-quantum cryptography standards established by international standardization organizations (e.g., NIST)
  6. Explore advanced cryptographic techniques (such as fully homomorphic encryption)
