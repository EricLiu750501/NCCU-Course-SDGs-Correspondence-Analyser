##  課程簡介Course Description
This course introduces the history of Arabic prose literature from the pre-Islamic period down to the end of the Abbasid period. The themes, styles, and characteristics of the prose pertaining to different regions and periods will be addressed, while selected texts will be read and analysed in depth.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By the end of the term, students will ideally be familiarised with the key features of Arabic prose and its history. Students will have read the most important prosaic texts representing different epochs and develop the ability to understand and engage critically with early Arabic prosaic texts.
