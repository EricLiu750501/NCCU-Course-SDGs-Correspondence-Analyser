##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程為針對華語零起點學生，協助初學者瞭解華語並對華語及華人文化產生興趣。課程從漢語拼音開始，課程內容以日常生活情境對話為主，培養初學者對華語聽、說 _、_ 讀、寫的初級能力。
This course is aimed at the beginning learners in Chinese language, helping beginners to understand Chinese and become interested in Chinese language and Chinese culture. The course starts with Chinese Pinyin, and the content of the course is mainly based on dialogue in daily life situations, cultivating beginners' primary abilities in Chinese listening, speaking, reading and writing.

2. Abilities to identify vocabulary and use it accurately
3. Basic interaction skills with others
