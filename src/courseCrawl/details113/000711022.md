##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
在本門課中，同學將學習以下內容  
1. Integration Techniques and applications of integration.  
2. Infinite sequences and series.Week 3:  
3. Vectors and vector functions.  
4. Partial derivatives.  
5. Multiple integrals.
