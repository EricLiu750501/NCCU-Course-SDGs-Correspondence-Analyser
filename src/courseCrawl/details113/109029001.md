##  課程簡介Course Description
The ability to design and implement technology-infused instruction has been recognized as an important prerequisite for teachers in the 21st century. Furthermore, content and language integrated learning (CLIL) has become an international trend and a national policy in Taiwan. Accordingly, adopting the spirits of CLIL, this course introduces emerging media technologies, the essence of bilingual education, and their tacit applications in situ. Homemade, modified, and collected free materials will be organized to enrich the content of a specific subject area, and the ASSURE instructional design model will be utilized as a roadmap to plan and carry out trial teaching in bilingual formats. Students are expected to gain knowledge and skills of developing bilingual teaching materials with educational media and technologies; also they will develop a pragmatic, realistic, and innovative mindset of media application in various educational contexts.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. Understand the definition, category, and historical development of educational media.
  2. Comprehend the essence of bilingual education and content and language integrated learning (CLIL).
  3. Explore and familiarize Top learning tools and their instructional applications.
  4. Search for open online educational resources and CLIL examples.
  5. Systematically design technology-enhanced bilingual teachings materials with the ASSURE model.
  6. Carry out bilingual ASSURE lesson plans and related media/materials in the classroom.
