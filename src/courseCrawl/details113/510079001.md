##  課程簡介Course Description
This course aims to provide students with the skills in Indonesian writing. In this course, the students will have opportunities to write various types of writing (explanatory, narratives, descriptives, argumentative, and persuasives writing) in personal, social, and business settings.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The course provides a wide range of new vocabulary and complex grammar to help students write in Indonesian. By the end of the course, students will be able to:
  * Read various types of writing in the Indonesian context
  * Write various types of writing in the Indonesian context
