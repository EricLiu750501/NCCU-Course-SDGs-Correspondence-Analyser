##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
1. 提供同學財產評價研究概念Provide students with an overview of state-of-art academic and professional research across various aspects of property valuation.
2. 教導同學國內外財產評價規範Provide students with knowledge about property valuation in terms of regulatory perspective both domestically and internationally.
3. 灌輸估價學術研究文章Instill students with ideas about real estate appraisal in terms of academic perspective.
4. 指導同學發表國際財產評價論文Guide participants to do research and write paper potentially for publication.
5.輔導同學如何成為合格不動產估價師Guide participants how to become eligible for real estate appraiser
