##  課程簡介Course Description
IMICS offers an internship course with credits in the spring semester. The internship must be related to communications. Abiding by the policy of the IMICS Program, the students taking this course must acquire prior approval from the Director, Professor Tammy Jih-Hsuan Lin before beginning the internship. Students may register for the internship course before or after completing the actual internship. But if a student does not complete the internship within the spring semester, he/she will receive an "incomplete" mark on the transcript.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The major objective of this course is to offer students the opportunity for practical training, including meaningful applications of and reflections on the classroom learning. Industry practices, such as news writing, filming, and website maintenance, are not only technical skills but require managerial minds to amplify the values of communication profession. Students can learn from a mutually reinforcing process where guidelines and theories are tested in practices, while practical experiences enlighten the rules suggested by research again. Therefore, students are expected to turn in a thoughtful report on their experiences and their learning at the end of the internship.
