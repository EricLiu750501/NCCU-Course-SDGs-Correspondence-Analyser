##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程[2學分，前12週授課，每次3小時]以企業經營管理實例，搭配獨特之實戰演練模組，結合基本的博奕（賽局）理論，針砭當前企業經營管理目標與決策模式。   
I reconstruct the many battles constantly faced by businesses in optimal decision making in this teaching project. The course employs Game Theory to analyze business practices. This course pays special attention to the factors that drive demand and supply changes and the ensuing responses to the outcomes. Then we will look into the business environment to derive conditions for sustaining cooperation and conditions for spurring competition.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
1. To enable the students understand corporate interaction as a game-theoretic problem.   
2. To familiar the students with the notions of game equilibrium and how it is analyzed and applied in practical cases.   
3. To embed a game-playing mentality within students through simulated business scenarios and decision making processes.
