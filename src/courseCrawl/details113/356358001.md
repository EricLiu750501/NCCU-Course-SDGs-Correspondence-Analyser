##  課程簡介Course Description
In this course, students will develop strong problem-solving skills in data science, focusing on real-world business applications. The curriculum covers essential Python libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and visualization tools, followed by an introductory exploration of Scikit-learn, the most widely used Python library for machine learning. 
Rather than relying solely on lectures, this course adopts a problem-solving-oriented teaching approach, emphasizing hands-on practice through continuous coding exercises and frequent in-class quizzes. This ensures that students not only grasp theoretical concepts but also learn to apply them effectively to solve complex data challenges. The two-stage training framework enhances their expertise in data preprocessing, data management, and analytics using Python and implementing machine learning algorithms to tackle business problems. 
By combining rigorous practice with structured problem-solving, this course prepares students to apply data science techniques in a business environment confidently. It equips them with the skills to analyze, interpret, and derive insights from real-world data.

By enrolling in this course, students will thoroughly comprehend diverse data science techniques and popular machine learning algorithms. Equipped with these abilities, students will be capable of adeptly addressing genuine business obstacles and honing their expertise in data science and business analytics.
