##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程採線上講授與實體實作並行，線上課程為理論部分，主要介紹基礎電子電路分析方法，涵蓋直流及交流電路分析、半導體電路元件，實體課程為實作各種基礎電子電路，例如放大器、濾波器、振盪器等
第一次課程介紹請到上課教室，線上課程部分，請選課同學用政治大學 g suite帳號 "學號"@g.nccu.edu.tw，加入google classroom課程網站 113電子電路學(代碼: 2evhcek)
https://classroom.google.com/c/NzQ4MzI2NTk5NTQ0?cjc=2evhcek 
This is a hybrid course with both online lecture and in class laboratory. Online lectures introduce basic concepts of electrical circuits. The contents include direct and alternative circuit analysis, characteristics of semiconductor electronic circuit. The in-class laboratory includes the implementation of several fundamental electronic circuits such as amplifier, filter, oscillator etc.
For the online lecture, please join the NCCU google classroom web site (to be announced) using student’s NCCU google suite account, which will be in the form of ID@g.nccu.edu.tw.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
認識機基本電子電路分析方法，並透過實作基礎電子電路了解如何各種電子元件的應用，修習此課程之學生將會學到初步電路分析方法理論，以及實作電路的經驗，並藉由整理實驗報告驗證理論和實作間的差異。
Learn preliminary knowledge about the electrical circuit analysis and understand how to implement the circuits through laboratory. The students will learn basic circuit analysis theory on electronic circuits and have experience in implementing the electronic circuit. The students will learn the difference between theory and laboratory through writing lab reports.
