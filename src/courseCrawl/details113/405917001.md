##  課程簡介Course Description
The course introduces the fundamental knowledge in public relations practices. The students learn about the role and the functions of public relations in strategic communication, mainly through the teaching/learning approaches of lectures, group discussions, student presentations, and readings.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
As an entry-level class for the public relations majors, the goal of the course is to establish students’ initial understanding of the profession. At the conclusion of the course, students will be expected to:
  1. Understand the role and functions of public relations in strategic communication.
  2. Have knowledge of the history of the profession and be aware of the industry trends.
  3. Understand the PR planning process. 
  4. Be aware of the ethics-related issues and codes of ethics in practice.
  5. Understand the basic concepts in media relations practices.
