##  課程簡介Course Description
  * 此課程與風管系大一風險管理課程設計不同。將以財管為基礎，了解金融業各項風險（包含利率風險、信用風險、市場風險等），加以衡量計算。將與FRM (金融風險管理師)考試相關。將以required textbook為準上課，且強烈建議每周練習課本後習題。
  * 本門課數量計算與報告分析並重。大三以上修習學生將以組為單位，選取金融業中值得探討之問題，由淺而深做兩次上台報告，每次報告時間約15到20分鐘。報告題目可針對金融業內任何風險與趨勢，在兩次報告中分析與討論。
  * 每周上課狀況預計為前兩小時課程，第三小時為分組報告時間（約二十分鐘）、討論時間（約十五分鐘），最後十五分鐘則預留給有意討論報告細節之同學。 (若當日有兩組報告則另計)

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * One midterm examination and one final exam will be given, and the 15% participation accounts for the participation in question for students. 
  * You will have a 35% oral presentation as a group during the semester. Each group is supposed to present twice in the class, with the first time focusing on broad introduction of one issue related to financial institutions (can be self-decided) and the second time focusing on detailed questions. Each time of the presentation should last around 20 minutes long, and questions last around 15-20 minutes. 
  * Students are encouraged to discuss the details of the report before handing in to the teacher.
  * Special Notice: for the welfare of the students in the class, cellular phone use is strictly prohibited! Please turn off your cellular phone before entering the class.
