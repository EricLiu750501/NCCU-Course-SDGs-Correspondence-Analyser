##  課程簡介Course Description
In the creative industries, creativity is at the core of job development and the growth of intellectual property is the focus. These industries incapsulate many aspects of the way cultures are represented domestically and around the world! For example: music and performing arts; traditional crafts; media and visual arts; video games and virtual reality; museums, galleries, and heritage; publishing and design; advertising and marketing; and creative tourism. 
The 3-credit course, which is conducted in English, explores this vast sector of the national and global economy by investigating events, facilities, and businesses in the NCCU/Wenshan vicinity. Guided by a task-based textbook to build knowledge and fluency of English language specific to a selection of creative industries, lessons include practical activities in and beyond the classroom. As a special feature of the course, students will have opportunities to engage with professionals to learn about potential careers in the creative industries with guest speakers and field trips. 
In this course, you will become familiar with the broad range of opportunities in the creative industries to discover career paths on your horizon. Because these industries are inherently global, learning and using the English language associated with them will bring broader access to experiences while you are in college and increase your prospects for the job market in the future

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This is an English for Specific Purposes (ESP) course designed to introduce students to the creative industries. The primary goal is for students to maximize their English language proficiency while exploring a wide range of topics in the creative industries. 
Learning outcomes comprise both language proficiency with cultural industry knowledge including:
  1. Language
     * Demonstrating improved proficiency in English language skills (reading, writing, speaking, and listening)
     * Using appropriate English vocabulary, grammar, and syntax relevant to creative industries
  2. Communication
     * Developing effective communication skills for presenting ideas, projects, and proposals confidently and persuasively in English
     * Demonstrating cultural sensitivity and awareness when working with individuals from different cultural backgrounds
  3. Industry knowledge
     * Acquiring fundamental knowledge of key concepts, trends, and practices within specific sectors of the creative industries
     * Learning industry-specific terminology, jargon, and concepts related to fields covered in class
  4. Critical thinking
     * Developing critical thinking skills to analyze and evaluate various aspects of the creative industries
     * Applying problem-solving strategies to address challenges and opportunities
