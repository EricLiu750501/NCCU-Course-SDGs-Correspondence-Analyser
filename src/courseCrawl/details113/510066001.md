##  課程簡介Course Description
This course aims to develop students' mastery of advanced Thai grammar and composition while fostering critical thinking and writing abilities.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By the end of the course, students will be able to
  * Use advanced Thai grammar correctly and
  * Compose various types of essays in Thai language.
