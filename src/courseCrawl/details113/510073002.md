##  課程簡介Course Description
Advanced Indonesian Conversation aims to develop students’ speaking skills in Bahasa Indonesia with a focus on expanding lexical resources, mastering a broader range of grammar, improving fluency, and refining pronunciation.
The course employs various interactive and engaging methods, including expressing individual opinions, pair dialogues, small group discussions, class presentations, and semi-impromptu activities designed to enhance spontaneous communication skills.
Students will explore advanced topics to deepen their understanding of specific Indonesian cultural and contextual settings.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The course aims to ground and strengthen students' speaking skills in Bahasa Indonesia. By the end of the course, students will be able to:
  * Express their opinions naturally and spontaneously on a wide range of topics in formal oral discourse, within the scope of the course.
  * Confidently initiate, sustain, and develop conversations on abstract rather than routine matters.
  * Communicate effectively and appropriately in professional, work-related environments.
  * Demonstrate a broader and deeper understanding of Indonesia’s socio-cultural contexts.
  * Utilize an upper-intermediate to advanced level vocabulary with confidence.
  * Deliver structured and engaging individual presentations in front of the class.
  * Effectively participate in and contribute to group discussions and presentations, navigating their dynamics and challenges.
