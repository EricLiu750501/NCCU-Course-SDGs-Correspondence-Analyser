##  課程簡介Course Description
This course introduces basic macroeconomics including measurement of national income, fiscal and monetary policies, and so on.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This introductory macroeconomic course will help you understand the world in which you live and make you a more astute participant in the economy. It will also give you a better understanding of the potential and limits of economic policy.
