##  課程簡介Course Description
A graduate-level course which embedding the students the ability to understand fundamental macroeconomic models (especially the Ramsey model) and to analyze macroeconomic phenomena.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students will be able to understand macroeconomic models and utilize the theory to analyze real-world phenomena.
Students will be able to have basic computation ability to numerically solve the theoretical model for quantitative analysis.
