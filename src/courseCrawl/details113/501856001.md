##  課程簡介Course Description
BREAKING NEWS! This course prepares students for their riveting careers in international journalism. Or for any other job really… Come join! 
This is an activity-based course with minimum "lecture" time. Students will get to explore topics that are important to them, explore their local community, and produce real new stories. 
This course develops one's professional writing skills, interview skills, public speaking skills advocacy/debate skills, networking skills, presentation skills, critical thinking skills, and problem-solving skills. 
Students will be assessed through in-class activities, and presentations.
Guest speakers may be invited to present on topics related to start-ups, human resources, and business communication.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students will be able to:
  * Master Interview Techniques: Gain proficiency in conducting professional interviews, including question formulation, active listening, and follow-up strategies.
  * Enhance Professional Writing Skills: Develop the ability to compose clear, concise, and impactful written communication suitable for international journalism environments.
  * Participate in Experiential Learning: Engage in in-class activities and real-world scenarios to practice and refine journalism-related skills.
  * Develop Networking Skills: Build confidence and techniques for effective networking in professional settings, both in-person and online.
  * Refine Presentation Skills: Deliver structured, engaging, and professional presentations tailored to diverse audiences.
  * Apply Critical Thinking: Analyze journalistic content critically and apply problem-solving strategies to address challenges in international journalism.
  * Engage with Industry Experts: Learn from guest speakers on topics such as start-ups, human resources, and business communication to gain practical insights into the field.
  * Prepare for International Careers: Build a comprehensive skill set to thrive in global journalism roles, focusing on adaptability, cultural awareness, and professionalism.
