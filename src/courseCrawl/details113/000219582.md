##  課程簡介Course Description
This is the second part of a one-year introductory course to economics. This semester we will focus on macroeconomics. Macroeconomics, according to Professor Mankiw, is the study of economy-wide phenomena. We will cover topics such as GDP, inflation, financial system as well as monetary and fiscal policy.
