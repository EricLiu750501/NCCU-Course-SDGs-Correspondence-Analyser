##  課程簡介Course Description
文物可分為有機材質與無機材質，而其中屬於戶外、半戶外空間的文物，多屬於無機材質，例如常見的有石質、壁畫、金屬、玻璃與陶瓷，雖具有較佳的耐候與材質性能，但如無適當的保存環境及方法，將造成不可逆性的損壞。基於文物保存之基礎建立，本課程全面的以無機材料為主軸，特開此學門課程。本課程分為初階、進階兩個階段完成教學。課程內容將建立無機材質的損壞現象調查、修護技術與案例分析為主軸，輔以實際修護技術演練與知識概念培養，以達成本課程目標。

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Cultural relics can be divided into organic and inorganic materials. Among them, cultural relics in outdoor and semi-outdoor spaces are mostly made of inorganic materials, such as stone, murals, metal, glass and ceramics. Although they have better weather resistance and material properties, However, without proper storage environment and methods, irreversible damage will occur. Based on the foundation of cultural relics preservation, this course is comprehensively centered on inorganic materials and this course is specially designed. This course is divided into two stages: beginner and advanced. The course content will be based on the investigation of damage phenomena of inorganic materials, repair techniques and case analysis, supplemented by actual repair technology drills and knowledge concept cultivation to achieve the objectives of this course.
