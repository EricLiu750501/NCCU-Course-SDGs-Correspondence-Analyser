##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is designed to equip students with practical skills in digital production and analysis, focusing on the integration of emerging technologies such as VR live streaming, AI-assisted multi-track real-time editing, and social media data analysis. It provides a comprehensive learning experience that combines conceptual instruction with hands-on practice. Students will learn to create digital content for new media, enhance audience engagement, and utilize data to support planning and evaluation processes. Emphasizing exploration, creativity, and practical application, this course aims to establish a strong foundation for students pursuing careers in digital media, marketing, and generative AI technology applications.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Gain an understanding of VR live streaming and AI-assisted editing, and attempt to complete digital content production.
  * Comprehend social media data analysis to develop strategic thinking and decision-making abilities.
  * Develop skills in digital project planning and execution while enhancing teamwork experience.
