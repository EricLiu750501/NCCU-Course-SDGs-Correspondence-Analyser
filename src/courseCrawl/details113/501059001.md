##  課程簡介Course Description
The purpose of this course is to learn the fascinating history of how English became the language that is used so widely all around the world today. We will learn about what is language and how humans are uniquely evolved to learn and use language. Then we will learn about the Indo-European language family of which English is a member. Next we will learn about the evolution of English on the British Isles as it transformed during the Old English Middle English and Modern English stages. Finally we will examine the status of English as a global language. Alongside this process of learning about the history of the English language, we will also expand our vocabulary by learning about words that entered the English language during these different periods, and by learning how the meanings of words have evolved over time .

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
After taking this course, you should:   
Understand the breadth of the History of English from Proto Indo-European to Present Day English   
Understand the basics of how languages ​​change over time   
Be exposed to the differences in the English language during Old, Middle, and Modern English periods   
Understand how Old English, Middle English, and Early Modern English have left remnants and affected Present Day English   
Understand the role English has as a global language   
Understand how English is continuing to change in the present   
Have an opinion on the role English should have in our present and future society
