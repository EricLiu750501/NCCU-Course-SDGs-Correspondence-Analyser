##  課程簡介Course Description
The topic of adolescent deviant behavior is a part of holistic human development. For secondary school teachers, it is essential to be well-versed in counseling skills for addressing deviant behaviors in adolescents. This course is conducted in English and combines elements of educational psychology, abnormal psychology, behavior modification techniques, and counseling philosophies. It aims to develop students' abilities to comprehensively assess adolescent behavioral issues, devise effective counseling strategies, and flexibly utilize various resources to assist in the mental development of adolescents. This approach helps in guiding them through challenges, removing obstacles, and leading towards a mature, balanced, flexible, and constructive personality. The course design integrates the spirit of STEAM education, emphasizing the five dimensions of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics. It incorporates the use of generative AI tools to create a flipped classroom learning environment that fosters knowledge acquisition, learning through doing, and problem-solving.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. Develop a basic understanding of adolescent psychological development and issues related to mental health.
  2. Enhance awareness and assessment skills regarding mental health and behavioral issues.
  3. Gain familiarity with adolescent psychological problems and counseling techniques.
  4. Adopt a problem-based learning approach, promoting self-directed learning through case studies, group discussions, brainstorming sessions, and similar methods.
