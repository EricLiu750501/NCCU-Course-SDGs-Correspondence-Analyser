##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. 學生能夠以中文簡單說明申請學校需要的手續及詞彙。
  2. 學生能夠以中文簡單談一談在新環境會碰到的困難及解決方法。
  3. 學生能夠以中文簡單介紹自己國家的地理位置、地理環境等概況。
  4. 學生能夠以中文簡單介紹自己國家的氣候及農產品出產概況。
  5. 學生能夠以中文簡單比較台灣和自己國家的地理環境、氣候及農產品的異同。
  6. 學生能夠以中文簡單比較台灣和自己國家的教育觀念和制度的異同。
```
1.Students can briefly explain in Chinese the procedures and vocabulary required for applying to schools.
2.Students can briefly talk about the difficulties they will encounter in the new environment and their solutions in Chinese.
3.Students can briefly introduce the geographical location and geographical environment of their country in Chinese.
4.Students can briefly introduce their country's climate and agricultural product production in Chinese.
5.Students can simply compare the similarities and differences in geographical environment, climate and agricultural products between Taiwan and their own country in Chinese.
6.Students can simply compare the similarities and differences between the educational concepts and systems in Taiwan and their own country in Chinese.
```
