##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is the first year of the two-year writing and reading program. It aims to increase students’ skills in academic writing and reading. For writing, students will learn the major rhetorical forms to write coherent essays. These strategies include narration, description, exposition, summary and response, etc. As to reading, this course aims to enhance students’ reading comprehension abilities. Reading skills to be taught include scanning, skimming, text analysis, identifying the main point and authors’ intention, differentiating facts and opinions, making inferences, and text appreciation. These skills will be taught via lectures, discussions, homework assignments, peer review, and individual conferences. Students are expected to become active and critical readers and writers through mastering these skills.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon completion of the course, students should be able to:
  1.     1. write coherent descriptive, narrative, expository, and summary-and-response essays; 
    2. use AI tools to enhance writing; 
    3. develop one’s reading-writing habits; and 
self-revise essays and respond to readers’ comments.
