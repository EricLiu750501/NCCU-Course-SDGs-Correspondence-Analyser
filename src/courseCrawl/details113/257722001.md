##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程主要藉由講授、學生分組討論及綠建築實地參訪，使學生了解綠建築及生態社區的專業知識。主要的課程內容包括綠色建築與生態社區之基礎概念以及智慧科技的運用，並以不同角度探討綠色建築與智慧生態社區之發展與未來。  
This course enables students to understand the professional knowledge of green buildings and ecological communities through lectures, student group discussions and field visits to green buildings. The main course content includes the basic concepts of green buildings and ecological communities and the application of smart technologies, and discusses the development and future of green buildings and smart ecological communities from different perspectives.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
使學生瞭解在氣候變遷下，於綠建築與智慧生態社區的面向中，以建築設計、運用智慧技術，材料運用等方式，達到減少廢棄物之排放、營造安全、舒適、環保以及低環境負荷的居住空間，使人、建築與環境生態能共生共榮，以達成追求永續發展之目標。  
To enable students to understand that under climate change, in the face of green buildings and smart ecological communities, through architectural design, the use of smart technology, material use, etc., to reduce waste emissions, create a safe, comfortable, environmentally friendly and low-environmental load environment. The living space enables people, buildings and the environment to coexist and prosper in order to achieve the goal of pursuing sustainable development.
