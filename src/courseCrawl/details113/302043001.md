##  課程簡介Course Description
This course studies several issues of monetary theory and policy with an emphasis on the conduct of monetary policy and its effects on the real economy and financial markets. Students should be familiar with microeconomics, macroeconomics, calculus (you must knowhow to solve optimization problems), and statistics (you must knowhow to analyze the data by appropriate methods).

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students should be able to analyze contemporary monetary theory and policy issues after taking this course. For example,
1. What are the implications of the link between inflation and the real economy for the conduct monetary policy?
2. How do the current monetary policy operations in the U.S. differ from the past?
3. How does monetary policy affect the real economy and financial markets through various channels?
