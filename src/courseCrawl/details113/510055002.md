##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is designed to develop students' foundational oral communication and listening skills in Thai. Students will acquire the ability to express themselves on familiar, everyday topics and engage in basic interactions relevant to routine situations.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Students will be able to understand the main ideas in dialogues and conversations related to familiar, everyday topics.
  * Students will be able to engage in short conversations and communicate effectively on topics pertinent to daily life.
  * Students will be able to articulate basic ideas, thoughts, and feelings about familiar subjects in everyday contexts.
