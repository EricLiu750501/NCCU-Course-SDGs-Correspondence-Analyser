##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is designed for students who have taken one-year Economics and related courses. The aim of the second semester is to provide students with fundamental knowledge regarding the role of monetary policies. Learning the economic rationale behind policy strategies is especially emphasized.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
In the second semester, we will explore the interplay between monetary policy and the financial market. In particular, we will focus on how the money supply is created within the banking system and the role of the central bank. Additionally, we will cover intriguing topics such as the implementation of non-conventional monetary policy tools, which will be discussed in class or assigned as case studies for further analysis after class.
