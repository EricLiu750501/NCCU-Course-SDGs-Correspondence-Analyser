##  課程簡介Course Description
The goal of this course is to impart, develop, and enhance graduate students’ understanding in leadership related research, theories, and literature. The course is designed to prepare students to comprehend and integrate broad and inter-related theoretical perspectives in contemporary leadership research. Students will also have the opportunity to develop their own unique and novel research questions in the field of leadership and learn how to properly study these research questions.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
1. Introduce fundamental concepts and theories in contemporary leadership research;  
2. Survey and review important leadership related research topics and theories;  
3. Apply existing theoretical perspectives in generating new research questions;  
4. Foster core skills to structure and plan out research proposals on leadership topics.
