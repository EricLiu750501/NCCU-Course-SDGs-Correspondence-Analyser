##  課程簡介Course Description
This course aims to introduce current events, current affairs and important issues discussed in the news media in Thailand. It also aims to expand students’ vocabulary with emphasis on specialized terminology on various topics in news. Students will express and exchanges their opinions on current events and current affairs in the selected news.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Students should be able to understand factual information in news
  * Students should be able to understand words frequently used in various types of news
  * Students should be able to express and exchange their opinions on current events and current affairs
