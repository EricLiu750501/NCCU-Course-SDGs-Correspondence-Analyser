##  課程簡介Course Description
作為一門英語授課、專為外國學生開設的國文通識，本課程將從跨文化視角，引領學生認識近代中國的思想與文化。本課程將分成兩個部分：
第一部分是關於近代中外學者及傳教士如何理解、如何向西方讀者再現中國的思想與文化，特別是儒家思想，其中涉及四位人物：理雅各、李佳白、辜鴻銘、林語堂。授課者將帶領學生們從他們的著作中反思：他們筆下的中國思想與文化有何差異？中外人士的立場與觀點有何異同？學生們將閱讀到一手文獻及當代研究，前者讓我們得以感受一百年前的思想世界，後者則從當代的學術眼光，回顧、檢視歷史人物的得與失、突破與局限。
第二部分是主題式的課程安排，依循政大中國語文通識的核心綱要，針對「自我叩問」、「人與人」、「人與自然」、「人與天」四大主題，共同研讀關於中國近代思想與文化的英文名著。針對這四大主題，師生將分別探究：女性自傳書寫、婚戀自由、素食與動物保護、靈學與扶乩實作等。
注意：本課程非華語學習性質，而是英語授課的近代中國思想與文化之通識課程。各週主題及內容將視同學們的學習情況及進度適時調整。
This course, taught in English and designed specifically for international students, provides a general introduction to Chinese thought and culture in modern China from a cross-cultural perspective. The course is divided into two parts:
The first part focuses on how Western missionaries and Chinese scholars at the turn of the century viewed Chinese thought and culture, particularly Confucianism, and how their perspectives differed. It examines four figures: James Legge, Gilbert Reid, Ku Hung-ming, and Lin Yutang. For this section, students will engage with primary sources and recent scholarship. The former allows students to immerse themselves in the intellectual world of a century ago, while the latter enables them to critically assess these historical figures' understandings, achievements, and limitations from a contemporary academic perspective.
The second part is organized thematically, covering such topics as "self-exploration," "interpersonal relations," "human and nature," and "human and Heaven." The readings are all focused on modern China and explore themes of autobiography, discourse of love, animal protection, and spirit-writing practices, respectively. 
Before each class, students are required to read the assigned texts and submit one comment and one question. The instructor will select suitable questions from the students' submissions to guide class discussions and reflections.
Note: This course is not a Chinese language course, but an English-taught general education course on modern Chinese thought and culture. The course content is subject to change based on students' needs and progress.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
1.使外國學生們對中國儒、釋、道三教思想有基本的瞭解。
2.使外國學生們認識在近代中國思想史上的幾位關鍵人物。
3.使外國學生們培養跨文化的比較視野及批判閱讀的能力。
1.To provide international students with a basic understanding of the three major Chinese teachings: Confucianism, Buddhism, and Taoism.
2.To introduce international students to several key figures in modern Chinese intellectual history.
3.To foster cross-cultural comparative perspectives among international students and strengthen their critical reading abilities.
