##  課程簡介Course Description
危險理論 (Risk Theory) is a cornerstone of actuarial science, addressing the modeling, quantification, and management of uncertainty in financial and insurance contexts. It provides the mathematical and probabilistic framework for evaluating risk-bearing entities' financial stability and solvency.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By the end of this module, candidates will be able to:
  1. Understand Risk Models :
     * Differentiate between frequency and severity models.
     * Describe aggregate risk models and their components.
     * Evaluate risk using stochastic methods and key probabilistic distributions.
  2. Apply Ruin Theory :
     * Analyze the probability of ruin for an insurer under varying financial conditions.
     * Understand the implications of surplus processes and premium strategies.
  3. Implement Premium Calculation Principles :
     * Derive and apply premium principles, including the expected value, variance loading, and other actuarial pricing models.
     * Balance equity, solvency, and profitability in premium designs.
  4. Measure and Manage Risk :
     * Calculate and interpret key risk measures, including Value at Risk (VaR) and Tail Value at Risk (TVaR).
     * Assess the impact of reinsurance and other risk mitigation techniques on portfolio stability.
