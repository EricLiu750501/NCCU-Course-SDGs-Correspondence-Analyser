##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course is designed to give students a working knowledge of the various types of fixed income securities, and conventions employed in the profession to value these securities. The course covers pure discount bonds, coupon bonds such as Treasury notes, and corporate bonds, floating rate notes, callable bonds, among many others, issued by public or private entities. We focus on bond pricing, yield curve construction, duration and convexity, and formal term structure models, and others. Therefore, much of the course is non-technical or employs only relatively simple algebra or calculus. Some aspects of the course, however, are quite technical, and require advanced techniques or concepts. We will explore some of these, and use Excel to develop working solutions.
