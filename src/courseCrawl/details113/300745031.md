##  課程簡介Course Description
課程簡介Course Description
The course is intended to be the continuation of Advanced Oral Training, in which we have already covered job search writing. So, 30 percent of this course focuses on oral communication, and the other 70 percent on written communication. 
The first module of the course is speech. Students will learn to enhance their fluency and articulation further, improve their language style, and effectively organize content to deliver a product-based Sales Pitch. In the second module, students learn to write essential business letters and common expressions in business operations.  Following that, students are introduced to how to write a statement of purpose. The fourth is to experience the trade fair, and the final module covers the business plan and the competition. Students will learn the structure and language style of a business proposal. In the final project, students will work in groups to present a business plan poster based on the business proposal, aiming to attract investment or a change in policy or practice.
