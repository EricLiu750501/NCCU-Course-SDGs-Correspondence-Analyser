##  課程簡介Course Description
This class is designed for a student’s first course in investments. The course emphasizes asset allocation as well as investment management and presents the practical applications of investment theories to convey insights of practical value. The major topics will include: (1) how markets work (2) the relation between risk and return (3) optimal portfolio selection (4) performance evaluation (5) fixed income securities (6) security analysis (7) derivatives markets.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The main objective of this class is to teach you these three elements: 
  1. Knowledge of portfolio theories, financial instruments, markets, and relevant models
Students should acquire and are familiar with knowledge of portfolio theories, financial instruments, markets, and the relevant models from the class
  1. Analytical skill
This class prepares students for familiarity with analytical skills in optimal portfolio management, the trade-off between return and risk, securities selection, and valuation.
  1. Practical application capability
The class teaches students to practice the application of investment theories to practical investment analysis and strategy.
