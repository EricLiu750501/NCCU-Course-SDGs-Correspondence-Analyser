##  課程簡介Course Description
The objective of this course is to provide students with the necessary skills to effectively implement appropriate methodologies when designing and conducting research. The course will offer a nuanced perspective on the development of dynamic capabilities of microenterprises, addressing the unique challenges and opportunities they confront in the face of grand challenges.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
• Investigate contemporary research and foundational theories regarding microenterprise strategy.  
• Determine the unique opportunities and obstacles encountered by microenterprises.  
• Acquire a thorough comprehension of the concept of dynamic capabilities for microenterprises from both the macro and micro perspectives.  
• Acquire an understanding of the research design and methodologies that are suitable for the examination of microenterprise contexts.
