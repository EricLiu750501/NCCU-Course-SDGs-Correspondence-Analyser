##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is an advanced course of ‘Introduction to Arabic Morphology’. In this course, students will review the morphological rules, with special emphasis on Arabic nouns and verbs, which are taught in ‘Introduction to Arabic Morphology’, and apply their knowledge of Arabic morphology to enhance their reading comprehension and writing skills. Through reading authentic texts in classical Arabic, they will practise the morphological conjugation and familiarise themselves with the extended (literal and metaphorical) meanings of various morphological forms of verbs and nouns.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By the end of this course, students (presumably) will be able to read complex texts in classical Arabic; improve their reading ability; comprehend unusual morphological forms; and conjugate verbs and nouns in accordance with contextual information.
