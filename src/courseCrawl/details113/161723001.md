##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程旨在介紹語音學（研究言語的科學）與音韻學（探討人類語言聲音模式的學科）的基本概念，並聚焦於華語的語音與音韻系統，特別是與二語習得相關的華語語音現象進行描述與分析。課程目標在於培養學生對言語行為進行分析的能力，包括運用國際音標符號來解析語音現象。此外，課程將介紹語音聲學分析的基本技術，幫助學生設計並執行針對特定語言現象的語音實驗。為提升實用性，課程中特別加入語音教學的實踐內容，重視口說與聽力訓練方法的探討與應用。學生將學習如何設計和實施有效的語音教學活動，如發音矯正、聲調訓練、音位辨識練習等，並探索提升聽力理解的策略，幫助不同母語背景的學習者克服語音學習的挑戰。此外，課程還將透過語料分析與語言問題的解決，討論語音學和音韻學中的重要議題，幫助學生深入了解世界語言聲音系統的異同，進一步提升其華語語音教學的專業能力。
This course provides an introduction to phonetics (the scientific study of speech sounds) and phonology (the study of sound patterns in human languages), with a focus on the phonetic and phonological systems of Mandarin Chinese. Special attention is given to analyzing and describing Mandarin phonetic features relevant to second language acquisition. The course aims to develop students’ analytical skills for investigating speech behavior, including the use of the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) to analyze phonetic phenomena. In addition, students will be introduced to basic techniques for acoustic analysis of speech, enabling them to design and conduct phonetic experiments targeting specific linguistic phenomena. To enhance practical applications, the course incorporates components of phonetic teaching, emphasizing oral and listening training methods. Students will learn how to design and implement effective phonetic teaching activities, such as pronunciation correction, tone training, and phoneme recognition exercises, as well as explore strategies for improving listening comprehension. These skills are tailored to help learners from diverse language backgrounds overcome challenges in acquiring Mandarin phonetics. Moreover, through corpus analysis and problem-solving tasks, the course will address key issues in phonetics and phonology. Students will gain a deeper understanding of the similarities and differences among the sound systems of various languages, equipping them with the professional skills needed for teaching Mandarin phonetics effectively.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程的主要目標是幫助學生深化華語語音學的相關知識並提升研究能力。在學習成效方面，一方面為從事華語文研究奠定扎實基礎，另一方面提升華語文教學的效能。課程強調語音教學能力的培養，特別是在口說與聽力訓練方面。學生將學習設計與實施有效的語音教學活動，進一步增強其在華語語音學研究與教學領域的專業能力。
The primary goal of this course is to help students deepen their knowledge of Mandarin phonetics and enhance their research skills. In terms of learning outcomes, the course aims to lay a solid foundation for conducting research in Mandarin linguistics while simultaneously improving the effectiveness of Mandarin language teaching. The course emphasizes the development of phonetic teaching skills, particularly in areas such as oral communication and listening training. Students will gain the ability to design and implement effective phonetic instruction, thereby enhancing both their research capacity and teaching proficiency in Mandarin phonetics.
