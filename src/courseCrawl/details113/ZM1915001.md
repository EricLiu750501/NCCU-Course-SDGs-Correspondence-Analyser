##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is specifically designed for GCIT students who either a) have never conducted research before, b) have never designed their own research before, or c) have conducted research before, but have never done so in one of the social sciences (i.e., communication, psychology, sociology, etc.). It takes a ground-up approach to give these students a comprehensive understanding of 1) what kinds of research designs are appropriate for a master’s level capstone or thesis, 2) how to prepare a research proposal, and 3) the basic components of a research paper. It is intended to be taken the semester before beginning a capstone project or thesis, although it can be taken earlier if the student so chooses. This course is NOT open to students from other departments or courses under any circumstances.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. Students should understand the core principles of ethics and practical and/or theoretical impact when it comes to study design.
  2. Students should be able to design a basic study on their own.
  3. Students should be able to craft a proposal that adequately explains the purpose, design, and importance of a project.
