##  課程簡介Course Description
This course aims to develop students’ reading strategies for coping with longer texts on general topics and some selected topics about Thailand. It also aims to expand the students’ essential and specialized vocabulary.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Students should be able to understand important information in longer texts on familiar and unfamiliar topics.
  * Students should be able to identify the meaning of unfamiliar words from the context.
