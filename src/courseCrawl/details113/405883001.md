##  課程簡介Course Description
廣告是行銷傳播的一環，涵蓋的範圍很廣，在我們的生活中無所不在。本課程將建構相關專業知識基礎，帶領同學動手執行消費者洞察分析，並根據分析結果發展廣告策略與創意，特別適合對廣告、行銷傳播、企劃、及實作有興趣的同學。
Advertising is a vital component of marketing communications, with a broad scope that permeates our daily lives. This course builds a solid foundation of professional knowledge, equipping students with the skills to conduct hands-on consumer insight analysis. Drawing on these insights, students will develop effective advertising strategies and creative concepts. The course is ideal for those interested in advertising, marketing communications, strategic planning, and practical execution.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程是廣告學的入門課程，希望幫助同學廣泛地了解廣告與行銷傳播的基本概念，包括品牌與消費者、企劃與策略、創意與設計、行銷傳播媒體、以及整合行銷傳播。這些基礎知識有助於同學們日後理解進階廣告課程，為未來在相關專業領域發展打下穩固的基礎。
This introductory course in advertising offers students a broad understanding of the core concepts in advertising and marketing communications. Key topics include branding and consumer behavior, strategic planning, creative development and design, marketing communication media, and integrated marketing communications. By building this foundational knowledge, the course prepares students for advanced studies in advertising and equips them with a solid base for future professional growth in related fields.

2. Attendance and Participation
  * If you are unable to attend a class, you must notify the professor via email before the class begins. Upon your return, you are required to provide valid documentation to formalize your absence. Alternative methods, such as having a friend inform the professor about your illness, will not be accepted. Leave requests must be supported by legitimate reasons; excuses such as travel or taking a driver’s license test will not be considered valid.
  * Attendance will be monitored sporadically. Arriving late to class will result in a one-point deduction from your final grade. Missing an entire class will incur a two-point deduction. Students who accumulate three absences will automatically fail the course.
3. Late Work Policy
  * 1 day late (i.e., immediately after the deadline up to 24 hours later): -10%
  * 2 days late (i.e., 24 to 48 hours after the deadline): -25%
  * 3 days late (i.e., 48 to 72 hours after the deadline): -50%
  * > 3 days late: -100% (I will not grade the assignment.)
4. Team Photo and Peer Evaluation
  * Each team for the term project will consist of five students.
  * Teams are required to submit a group photo that includes the team number and the names of all members. A bonus point will be added to the final grade for teams that submit their photo on time.
  * At the end of the semester, a peer evaluation process will take place. Your final grade may be adjusted based on the results of these evaluations. Poor peer evaluation scores, particularly when compared to high-performing teammates, may result in failing the course. This highlights the importance of active, consistent participation and meaningful contributions to the team's efforts, both in class and outside of it.
