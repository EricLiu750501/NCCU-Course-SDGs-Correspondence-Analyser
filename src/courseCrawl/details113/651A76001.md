  1. Use of trusts in investments 投資型信託
  1. Forms and Structures 形式和結構 (6 hours)
  1. trusts as collective investment vehicles 信託作為集體投資工具(CIS)
  2. classification of investment vehicles: private funds, listed funds, real estate investment, trusts, fund of funds, umbrella funds, master-feeder funds, pension funds投資工具的分類：私人基金、上市基金、房地產投資信託基金、組合型基金、傘形基金、連結式基金、養老基金
  3. Dissecting a trust deed and offering/placement memorandum of investment trusts剖析信託契約及募集備忘錄
  4. discussion of subscriptions, redemption, transfer, roles of trustee, manager, prime broker, custodian and transfer agent and other key terms in the trusts 討論認購、贖回、轉讓、受託人、基金經理、主要經紀人、託管人及轉讓代理人的角色及信託的其他主要條款
  1. Investment funds related tax planning and financial regulations投資基金相關的稅收籌畫和金融業監管規定 (3 hours)
  1. Taxation of CIS, fund manager compensation (capital gains/income, transfer pricing issues), distribution to investors集體投資工具、基金經理薪酬（資本收益/收入、轉讓定價問題）、投資者分配的稅收
  2. Example: UK Venture Capital Trust 示例：英國風險投資信託基金
  3. Financial regulations of key onshore and offshore jurisdictions related to CIS, fund manager and distribution/offering of interests in the CIS主要在岸和離岸法律管轄區與集體投資工具、基金經理和集體投資工具發售有關的金融業監管規定
  1. Trust jurisdictional comparison 信託法律管轄區比較(3 hours)
  1. Onshore vs offshore jurisdictions – key differences and impacts在岸與離岸法律管轄區 - 主要差異和使用效果
  2. Examples of key mid-shore/offshore jurisdictions: Asian – Singapore, Taiwan, Hong Kong, PRC, New Zealand, Cook Islands; Caribbean – BVI, Cayman, Bermuda and theBahamas; Channels Islands – Jersey and Guernsey; Mediterranean – Malta and Cyprus; African – Mauritius and Seychelles)近岸/離岸法律管轄區例子：亞洲-新加坡、臺灣、香港、中國、紐西蘭、科克群島; 加勒比海地區-英屬維京群島、開曼群島、百慕達和巴哈馬群島; 海峽群島-澤西島和根西島; 地中海-馬爾他和塞浦路斯；非洲–毛里求斯和塞席爾）
  3. Comparisons of key jurisdictions 主要法律管轄區比較
  4. Helping clients choose trust jurisdictions協助客戶選擇信託法律管轄區
  1. Family trust and family office 家族信託和家族辦公室
  1. Overview of family trusts and family office 家族信託和家族辦公室概況(6 hours)
  1. Introduction to family trusts家族信託簡介
  2. Reasons for setting up family trusts建立家族信託的理由
  3. Typical family trust structures典型家族信託結構
  4. Family trust products家族信託產品
  5. Introduction to family offices家族辦公室簡介
  6. Types of family offices 家族辦公室類型
  7. Typical family office structures典型家族辦公室結構
  1. Family trust and family office related tax planning and financial regulations issues家族信託和家族辦公室相關的稅收籌畫和金融業監管規定(3 hours)
  1. Tax planning with family trusts – general principles including tax savings, tax deferral, tax residency and other tax issues throughout the lifecycle of a trust, and examples透過家族信託進行稅收籌畫 - 一般原則包括稅收減免、稅收延期、稅務居住地和其他信託生命週期中的稅務問題，以及示例
  2. Global tax issues related to family office與家族辦公室有關的全球稅務問題
  3. Global family office financial regulations家族辦公室全球金融業監管規定

  1. Introduction and techniques 介紹和技巧(3 hours)
  1. Introduction to practical case studies實際案例研究簡介

  3. Assignment and grouping 派發案例和分組
  4. Research, writing and presentation skills 研究、寫作和演講技巧
