##  課程簡介Course Description
The purpose of this course is to introduce students to the theory and applications of Markov chains. Topics include the fundamentals of Markov chains, transition matrices, classification of states, and applications. The course will include both theoretical concepts and practical examples, and students will gain experience in analyzing and solving problems related to Markov chains.
This is an EMI course, in which English is used as the primary language for teaching and learning.
Prerequisites : Calculus and basic Linear Algebra

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By the end of this course, students will be able to:
  1. Understand the foundational concepts of Markov chains and their properties.
  2. Construct the transition matrix for a given Markov chain. 
  3. Apply the concepts of transition matrices, state classification, and steady-state analysis.
  4. Solve practical problems involving discrete Markov chains.
  5. Analyze real-world systems modeled by Markov chains.
  6. Communicate and express their ideas in English, including listening, reading, speaking, and writing.
