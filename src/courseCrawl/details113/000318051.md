##  課程簡介Course Description
This course covers the accounting treatments for key topics in basic financial accounting, including:
1. Accounting treatments for investing activities, encompassing plant assets, natural resources, and intangible assets, as well as the investment of debt and equity securities, etc.
2. Accounting treatments of financing activities, covering short-term and long-term liabilities, equity, etc.
3. Preparation of the cash flow statement.
4. Analysis of financial statements.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
1. Comprehend the role of accounting information and financial statements in the capital market.
2. Acquire familiarity with the various procedures and steps of the accounting cycle.
3. Grasp the accounting treatments for investing and financing activities.
4. Integrate IT applications and visualization technology to comprehend accounting issues, thereby achieving the goals of AACSB through practical learning.

Course Requirements:
1. This course is exclusively for students from the Accounting Department (Decision made by the departmental meeting of the Accounting Department).
2. Given the need to cover 7 chapters plus an Appendix within 15 weeks, additional time before or after class may be required.
3. To maintain teaching quality, the course scope is determined by the Financial Accounting Ad Hoc Committee meeting of the Department of Accounting.
4. It is your responsibility to preview the chapter materials before class and make an effort to complete all exercises and problems at the end of each chapter afterward. Based on prior experience, students who have completed exercises and problems performed significantly better than those who did not.
5. Absence is not permitted for mid-term and final exams, unless university-approved official leave is granted.
6. Please refrain from copying assignment answers from other students; otherwise, your homework score will be zero. Late assignments will also be counted as a zero score.
7. The final total score will not be adjusted. Requests for adjustment after the final exam will result in a deduction of 10 points.
