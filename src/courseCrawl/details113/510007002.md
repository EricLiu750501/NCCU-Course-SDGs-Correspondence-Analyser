##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程延續中級越語會話課程I，幫助學生掌握越南語中級水準，並加深對越南文化的理解。本課程以發展學生溝通能力為主，包含增加字彙數量並提高越南語口語的流利程度。
The course is a continuation of intermediate conversational Vietnamese I, which helps build students’ Vietnamese proficiency at the upper-intermediate level and deepen their understanding of Vietnam culture. This course aims to further develop the students’ conversational abilities, including enlarging vocabulary and enhancing fluency in spoken Vietnamese.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程著重加強越南語會話技巧的穩固基礎，運用增加詞彙、語法，學生將能參與各種話題的交流。選修本課程，學生將建立以下基礎：
This course aims to reinforce a solid foundation in Vietnamese conversational skills by providing extended vocabulary and grammar of spoken language, the students will be able to participate in engaging conversations on a variety of topics. By the end of the semester, students will obtain as follow:
發音 Pronunciation:
  * 運用越南語的自然聲調習得標準發音。
Obtain the perfect pronunciation with natural intonation in Vietnamese language.
詞彙 Vocabulary:
  * 增加口語說法上的詞彙。
Obtain extended vocabulary in spoken language.
  * 獲取越南俚語、文化、習俗與傳統中的特定詞彙。
Gain specific vocabulary with regard to Vietnamese slangs, customs, culture, and traditions.
  * 能辨識同音異義詞。
Be able to distinguish homonyms.
  * 能適當運用同義詞和反義詞。
Be able to use effectively synonyms and antonyms.
  * 辨認純越詞、漢越詞、外來詞或混種詞等詞彙類型之不同處。
Distinguish types of vocabulary which are pure Vietnamese or Sino-Vietnamese or loanwords or mixed words.
文法 Grammar:
  * 習得進階混合句、片語的用法，如 “ biết chừng nào”, may mà…không thì”, “…làm sao mà…được”, “suýt nữa”, “A+ đến mức B”…
Obtain the addition of mixed sentences, the usage of complex phrasesfor example- “ biết chừng nào”, may mà…không thì”, “…làm sao mà…được”, “suýt nữa”, “A+ đến mức B”…
  * 掌握動詞，助詞，字首，副詞，形容詞的各種補語。
Acquire a variety of knowledge of verbs, front elements, adverbs, and adjectives.
聽力 Listening:
  * 能夠傾聽和理解連貫的對話，理解在各種情況下表達方式的差異，當中包含初級越語, 初級越語會話，中級越語, 中級越語會話範圍中的詞彙和語法。
Be able to listen and comprehend coherent conversations; understand differences of diverse expressions in a variety of situations within the limits of vocabulary and grammar.
口說 Speaking:
  * 能夠運用自然語調和流利的越語，獲得足夠的口說能力，在不同的專業領域進行溝通。
Be able to acquire speaking proficiency sufficient to communicate in different professional areas with natural intonation and fluency in Vietnamese.
閱讀 Reading:
  * 閱讀和辨識各個主題中更長且複雜的口語表達意思。
Read and identify the purpose of longer spoken texts with complexity in various topics.
寫作 Writing:
  * 寫出有效傳遞特定主題、文化資訊之文章，例如，風俗與習慣，跨文化交際，地區文化相關的文章。
Write articles effectively transmit cultural information on particular topics-for example, Vietnamese customs, and habits, Cross-Cultural Communication, Cultural Regions…
文化知識 Cultural knowledge:
  * 使用中高級越語獲得關於傳統活動、社區等文化知識。
Acquire cultural knowledge through the use of Vietnamese at the upper-intermediate level with regards to traditional activities, community....
越語檢定標準 Vietnamese language proficiency level:
  * 達到B2(中高級標準)
To reach B2(Upper-intermediate)
