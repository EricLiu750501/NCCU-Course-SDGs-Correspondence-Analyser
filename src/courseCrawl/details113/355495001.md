##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The objective of this course is to help Ph.D. students navigate theories and studies of meaning and sensemaking in and around organizations. Students will:
  * Learn about theories of meaning and sensemaking in organization and management literature.
  * Develop research ideas relevant to their own research interests.
  * Communicate to an academic audience through written reports and oral presentations.
