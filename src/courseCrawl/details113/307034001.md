##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is aimed to give students an overview of theoretical and empirical contributions of modern corporate finance, and help them to make financial decisions. Various important topics of finance are included, such as valuation, equity offerings, payout policy, financial leverage, and mergers and acquisitions.
This course is extremely challenging and time-consuming. It is advised that students should not take this course if they cannot spend time in preparing the coursework. Students are required to work hard to finish readings, cases and the term project in the semester. This course is open to finance major (or minor) students only.
此課程僅開放給財管系、雙主修財管及輔系財管的同學，全班上限 56 名。因教室大小受限，不開放給外系、所同學，請勿來信或來電詢問。
