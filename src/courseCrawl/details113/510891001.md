##  課程簡介Course Description
《泰文閱讀（三）》旨在提升學生閱讀泰文故事與短篇小說的能力，選讀內容包括以泰文書寫的伊索寓言和短篇小說。本課程著重培養學生對故事情節的理解及文學敘事手法的分析能力，為未來學習泰國文學課程奠定扎實基礎。本課程不限於已修讀《泰文閱讀（一）》或《泰文閱讀（二）》的學生，只要具備基本泰文閱讀能力者皆可選修。
"Thai Reading III" aims to enhance students' ability to read Thai stories and short novels, focusing on texts such as Aesop's fables and short stories written in Thai. This course emphasizes developing students' understanding of storylines and analytical skills in literary narration, laying a solid foundation for future studies in Thai literature. The course is not limited to students who have completed "Thai Reading I" or "Thai Reading II." It is open to anyone with basic Thai reading skills.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * 閱讀能力提升：能流暢閱讀並理解泰文書寫的寓言故事與短篇小說，掌握文本的核心思想與細節。
  * 文學素養養成：熟悉寓言故事及短篇小說的基本特徵，具備初步的文學分析能力。
  * 批判思維訓練：能就作品中的角色設定、情節發展及文化意涵進行深入思考與討論。
  * 語言能力拓展：擴充學生的泰語詞彙量，提升對句型結構的理解，增強泰文閱讀的精準性與深度。
  * 跨文化理解：透過文本中的價值觀與文化象徵，深化對泰國文化背景及社會觀念的認識。
  * Improvement in Reading Skills: Achieve fluent reading and comprehension of Thai-written fables and short stories, grasping the core ideas and details of the texts.
  * Development of Literary Acumen: Familiarize students with the basic characteristics of fables and short stories and equip them with preliminary literary analysis skills.
  * Critical Thinking Training: Engage in in-depth thinking and discussions on character development, plot progression, and cultural meanings in the works.
  * Expansion of Language Skills: Enrich students' Thai vocabulary, enhance their understanding of sentence structures, and improve the precision and depth of their Thai reading skills.
  * Cross-Cultural Understanding: Deepen awareness of Thai cultural background and social values through the symbolic meanings and values presented in the texts.
