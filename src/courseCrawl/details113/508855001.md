##  課程簡介Course Description
The introductions of the political and economic situations of the current Republic of Turkey. Furthermore, to discuss the relations of Turkey and other European countries.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Develop students 'insight into current affairs and enhance students' understanding of Turkey and Europe. At the same time, students are taught the ability to analyze and express themselves.
