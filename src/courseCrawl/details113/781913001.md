##  課程簡介Course Description
請注意：此門課程將以英語授課，所有的報告與討論也將以英文進行。本課程強烈建議與《機器人與AI 互動專題：理論與研究》同時修習，會對HRI與HAII的觀點有更完整的認知，有助於設計專案的發想與聚焦。
Note: This course will be taught in English. All presentations and discussions will also be conducted in English. It is strongly recommended to take this course together with the “Seminar on Robot and AI Interaction: Theories and Research” in order to gain a more comprehensive understanding of perspectives in Human-Robot Interaction and Human-AI Interaction, which will help in generating and refining design project ideas.
This project-based course focuses on the design and development of Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) and Human-AI Interaction (HAI). Students will engage in hands-on projects to conceptualize, prototype, and evaluate interactive systems such as conversational AI agents and performative robots. Students will explore design principles that enhance usability, functionality, and user experience (UX). The course emphasizes practical skills in designing for interaction while fostering creativity and critical thinking about the role of intelligent agents in our daily life and work settings.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By the end of this course, students will:
  * Understand design principles for HRI and HAII.  
Learn fundamental concepts and frameworks for creating interactive systems.
  * Develop practical design skills.  
Create, prototype, and test interaction designs.
  * Enhance teamwork and project management abilities.  
Collaborate on projects to create designs of intelligent agents.
  * Evaluate user experiences and system performance.  
Use methods to assess the effectiveness and usability of interactive systems.
  * Explore ethical and societal implications of design.  
Reflect on how interaction designs impact users and society at large.
