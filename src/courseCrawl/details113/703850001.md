##  課程簡介Course Description
This course begins with a brief introduction to various social media (SM) platforms, highlighting their diverse social purposes. Then, the course provides a comprehensive overview of the data sources and types obtained from these platforms, followed by an in-depth discussion on data collection and analysis methods. Weekly hands-on sessions are provided, supplemented by individual assignments, term exams, and group projects. The course aims to prepare students for careers as data scientists or social commerce marketers, fostering an ethical mindset throughout their learning journey. 
Note: The initial design of the course syllabus (though not the very first) was introduced in Fall 2017 and later revised in Spring 2023, with both versions delivered in Mandarin. Please see two links at the end of this page for those syllabi. In Spring 2025, the course will be offered as one of the EMI courses at NCCU. Please also refer to the selected online learning resources listed at the end of this page to deepen your knowledge of Social Network Analysis (SNA) and enhance your technical skills for analyzing various types of social media (SM) data.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Students can develop an understanding of the data attributes collected from various social network platforms.
  * Students can acquire the skills to ethically conduct a wide range of social network data analyses.
  * Students possessing diverse perspectives can collaborate with others to explore insights from social network analysis results through group projects.
