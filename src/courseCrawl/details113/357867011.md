##  課程簡介Course Description
Mergers and acquisitions are common strategies undertaken to achieve the strategic goals of corporations. Practitioners and academics have addressed a significant number of issues on M&As. Along with global market development, cross-border deals attract great attention from academic researchers. In addition to the motives of cross-border M&As, both macro- and micro-economic factors greatly affect the deals’ successes. The non-economic implications of cross-border M&As also deserve attention.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This class intends to provide students with an understanding of cross-border mergers and acquisitions (CBM&As). Instead of traditional lecturing, this class will be conducted as an interactive class. Both students and the instructor will determine the subjects for class discussions and play active roles in leading the class discussion. The purposes of this class design are to build up the capability of logical thinking and independent study. The maximum enrollment is 8
