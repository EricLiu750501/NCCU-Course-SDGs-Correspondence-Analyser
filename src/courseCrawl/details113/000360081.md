##  課程簡介Course Description
```
The objective of this course is to provide the students with the essential and fundamental concepts of elementary statistics. The topics include descriptive statistics, probability, sampling, design of experiments, and statistical inferences. At the end of this course, the students should be able to define the real problem, to use graphical and numerical summaries, to apply standard statistical inference procedures, and to draw conclusions from statistical analysis. In addition to materials in the textbook, the students are required to use the laptop or computer lab for some class activities.
```
