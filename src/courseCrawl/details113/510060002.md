##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is designed to develop students’ advanced oral communication skills in Thai. Students will build academic and technical vocabulary across various topics, with a primary focus on Thai culture and society. Through interactive activities, they will exchange facts and articulate opinions on subjects within and beyond their areas of interest.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Students will demonstrate a clear understanding of academic and technical vocabulary, phrases, and expressions related to Thai culture and society.
  * Students will accurately use academic and technical vocabulary, phrases, and expressions in contexts related to Thai culture and society.
  * Students will effectively express facts and articulate opinions in a manner appropriate to the Thai cultural and societal context.
  * Students will engage in discussions and debates on topics related to Thai culture and society, using appropriate language and expressions.
  * Students will effectively use technology to research and gather information on assigned topics.
