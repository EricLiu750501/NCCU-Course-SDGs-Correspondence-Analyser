##  課程簡介Course Description
Students will study about the formation of traditional Chinese drama and oral performance from their origins in early Chinese history to their struggle for survival in the present day. These include Yuan zaju, Ming and Qing chuanqi, and Beijing opera. Students will also study modern Chinese drama and oral performance, which includes modern Chinese drama of the twentieth century and representative contemporary plays in Taiwan.
學生將學習到傳統中國戲劇表演的形成，自早期中國歷史時期直到今日的變化圖強。這些包括元雜劇，明清傳奇和京戲。學生也會學習現代中國戲劇表演藝術，包括二十世紀的中國戲劇及當代台灣代表戲劇。

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course focuses on the survey of traditional and modern Chinese drama and oral performance. The course will be conducted in English, and required readings are also in English. In addition to studying English, students are expected to express themselves in English in introducing Chinese culture and Chinese drama.
本課程專注在傳統與現代中國戲劇表演藝術。本課程將以英語授課，閱讀教材亦以英文為主。學生不但學習英語，還可以用英文表達介紹中國文化及中國戲劇。
