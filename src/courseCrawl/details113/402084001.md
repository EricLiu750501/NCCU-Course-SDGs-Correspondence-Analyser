##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon completion of this course, you should be able to:
  * Understand the contemporary media planning environment through discussions of current events, trends, controversies, and ethical dimensions.
  * Know the language and terminology related to strategic media planning and activation.
  * Be familiar with the resources, data, and research tools available for advertising media planning.
  * Gain conceptual knowledge of media industries, the relationships between those institutions, media types/vehicles, and other contact points available to reach diverse audiences.
  * Understand how advertising integrates with other promotional tools to create effective marketing communication plans.
  * Develop an understanding of traditional, digital, social, mobile, and other emerging media, and their uses as advertising vehicles.
  * Effectively communicate media planning concepts and recommendations in both written and verbal forms.
  * Be able to effectively use the critical thinking skills involved in the media planning process.
