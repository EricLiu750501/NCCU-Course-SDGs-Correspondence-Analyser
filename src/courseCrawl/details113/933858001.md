##  課程簡介Course Description
This course explores the evolution of commerce, transitioning from traditional e-commerce to the emerging field of virtual commerce (V-commerce). Students will analyze how digital and virtual experiences are reshaping customer journeys, focusing on checkout processes and digital payment solutions. Through case studies from global platforms like Zara, Amazon, SHEIN, Gucci, and Grab, students will gain insights into successful strategies that improve user experience and optimize the path to purchase. The course features interactive discussions, real-world case studies, and group projects to deepen understanding of V-commerce concepts.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The focus will be on how digital and virtual experiences are revolutionizing customer journeys, particularly during the checkout process and digital payment solutions. By the end of this course, students will be able to critically evaluate the impact of digital and virtual technologies on customer experiences and purchasing behavior. They will gain practical knowledge of V-commerce strategies and digital payment solutions and apply this understanding through case studies and group projects to propose solutions for enhancing e-commerce platforms.
