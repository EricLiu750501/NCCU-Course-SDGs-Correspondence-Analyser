##  課程簡介Course Description
This course will explore the theories and practices of international financial management, emphasizing exchange rate parity conditions, determinants of corporate hedging, cross-listing decisions, and topics related to international financing decisions.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
By applying the financial concepts learned in this course, students will gain insight into the decision-making processes of financial managers in multinational firms. Course materials include mini-cases at the end of each chapter and cases/papers on related topics.
