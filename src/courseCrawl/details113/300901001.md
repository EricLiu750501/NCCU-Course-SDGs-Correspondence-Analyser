##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is the second part of Intermediate Microeconomics. As an advanced course in undergraduate microeconomics sequence, formal models and current topics are focal points.
This course mainly consists of three parts. The first part discusses revealed preference, public goods, externalities, and public choice. The second part introduces the game theory, including strategic and extensive form games with complete and incomplete information. Finally, in the third part, we will introduce some classic applications such as the labor market, insurance market, auction, and contest.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The goals of this course are the following:
  1. Provide students with a comprehensive understanding of microeconomics. 
  2. Develop the analytical approach for microeconomics issues. 
  3. Pave the way for further graduate studies in economics/management.
