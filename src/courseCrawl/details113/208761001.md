##  課程簡介Course Description
This course focuses on helping students gain a working understanding of econometrics. I will introduce econometric tools to identify causal effects and have a substantial focus on real-world problems. The problem sets will include both theoretical questions and statistical analysis of data, which will involve the software package STATA and R.
