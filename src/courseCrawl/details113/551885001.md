##  課程簡介Course Description
This course will strive to provide TESOL students with a conceptual understanding of statistical procedures needed (1) understand statistical concepts, (2) to analyze the data (3) to read and understand TESOL/linguistics statistical reports and (4) to do TESOL/linguistic quantitative research. The materials covered in this course will try to span most of TESOL/linguistics research methods so that students can make a connection between statistics and their research interests. Students are not expected to have previous background in statistics or R. A gentle introduction to R will be given at the beginning of the class and along the semester, students are expected to master using R with different topics.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon semester completion, students will: 
  1. know what descriptive and inferential statistics are good for, 
  1. know the fundamental terms and concepts of statistics, 
  1. know the requirements for frequently occurring statistical tests, 
  1. be able to implement frequently occurring statistical tests with R, a program for statistics, 
  1. be able to interpret the outcome of statistical tests and can visualize/verbalize the results.
