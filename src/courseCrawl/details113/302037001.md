##  課程簡介Course Description
This course will introduce the big picture of international financial environment, including multinational firms, countries' balance of payment, and international financial markets. This course will also introduce the foreign exchange risk and how to hedge that.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course aims at giving a broad understanding of what International Finance is, what are the factors that can affect the financial returns of a multinational firm and what can the firm do to diminish its exposure to these factors.
