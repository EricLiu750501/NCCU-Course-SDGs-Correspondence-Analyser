##  課程簡介Course Description
The objective of this course is to provide the students with the essential and fundamental concepts of statistics. Based on the foundation in Statistics (I), the course in this semester covers inference of two population means and variances, chi-squared test, ANOVA, regression, forecasting, and other applications.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course is designed to introduce college-level students the basic concepts in statistics. The objective is to equip students with consequently requisite quantitative skills that they can employ and build on in flexible ways. Students will develop statistical thinking and sills in exploratory data analysis; understand fundamental theories for statistical inference; analyze various types of data appropriately and draw conclusions from statistical analyses that help making right decisions.
