##  課程簡介Course Description
The main purpose of this seminar is to introduce doctoral students to the fundamental theories and issues in the organization theory (OT) literature. After completing the course, students will acquire a basic understanding of the major subject areas in the field of OT and be able to apply them in their research endeavors.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Understand the major subject areas in the OT literature.
  * Understand how to evaluate OT research papers in terms of research questions, key assumptions, theoretical development, critical findings, and limitations.
  * Develop the ability to formulate proper research questions, develop testable hypotheses or propositions, and design empirical research.
