##  課程簡介Course Description
This course intends to provide students with a general idea about the law of contracts under the scope of common law system. We will begin talking from the basic contract law rules to further discuss how to apply those rules to a practical case. This course will also introduce the laws of wills, trusts, and the U.S. federal civil procedure which are essential knowledge and skills an international lawyer must be equipped with.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
You will grasp a general idea about the law of contracts under the scope of common law system. You will understand the rules of UCC Article 2 and other important concepts. You will realize the basic contract law rules and understand how to apply those rules to a practical case. You will be equipped with the fundamental skills to deal with complicated cases involving cross-border legal issues.
