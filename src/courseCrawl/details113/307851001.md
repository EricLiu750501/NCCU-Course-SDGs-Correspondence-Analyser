能力項目說明
  * A. 溝通能力 Students will acquire practical professional skills – communication
  * B. 理解與辨別社會責任與企業倫理及永續議題，並在未來工作中運用企業倫理概念的能力 Students will understand and be able to identify ethical/social responsibility/sustainability issues and apply ethics to business decisions
  * C. 具備商學基礎知識與能力&財務領域專業知識 Students will gain basic & professional knowledge in financial field
