##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程延續中級越語課程I、進一步發掘聽、說、讀、寫的中階技巧與更深入的越南文化。本課程著重於現今越南日常生活的各個面向、提供給學生穩固的越南語基礎。
The course is a continuation of intermediate Vietnamese I, which is further developing the integrated skills of listening, speaking, reading, and writing at upper intermediate level, together with a deeper understanding of Vietnam culture. The lessons have focused on various aspects of the life of present-day Vietnam, in order to provide students with a solid foundation in using Vietnamese.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程將運用大量新詞彙和文法掌握的延伸，有效幫助學生運用口說及寫作交流廣泛的議題。選修本課程，學生將建立以下基礎：
The course provides a wide range of new vocabulary and an extended grasp of grammar with the aim to help students use Vietnamese effectively on a broad range of topics in both oral and written communication. On the completion of this course, students will be able to:
發音 Pronunciation:
  * 以擁有完美的越南語發音為目標。
Obtain the goal of perfect Vietnamese language pronunciation.
詞彙 Vocabulary:
  * 增加口說與寫作詞彙量
Obtain extended vocabulary in both spoken and written language.
  * 獲得學術、文化和社會中運用的新詞彙。
Gain new vocabulary used in academic learning, culture, and society.
  * 辨認純越語、漢越詞、外來詞或混種詞等詞彙類型之不同處。
Distinguish types of vocabulary which are pure Vietnamese or Sino-Vietnamese or loanwords or mixed words.
文法 Grammar:
  * 掌握片語與混合句之用法，如 “...cùng với …làm cho ”, “muốn…thì phải ”, “không thể…mà…thiếu… ”, “Không phải chỉ vì… mà là vì…” 等
Acquire knowledge of the usage of complex phrases and mixed sentences -for example, “...cùng với …làm cho… ”, “muốn…thì phải… ”, “không thể…mà…thiếu… ”, “không phải chỉ vì… mà là vì… ”
  * 於各式課文中, 運用專有詞彙及豐富的詞法與句法，如字首“đáng, mối …”，片語“bằng chứng là, không tránh khỏi, gắn liền với, liên quan đến… 等。
Gain the use of specialized words and intensive knowledge of morphology and syntax which are introduced in different texts of readings- for example prefix “ mối, đáng… ”; phrase “bằng chứng là, không tránh khỏi, gắn liền với, liên quan đến… ”
聽力 Listening:
  * 能夠傾聽和理解在各種情況下的連貫對話，包含初級越語，初級越語會話，中級越語會話中級越語範圍中的詞彙和語法。
Be able to listen and comprehend coherent conversations in diverse situations within the limits of vocabulary and grammar.
口說 Speaking:
  * 運用準確的發音和自然的表達，習得交流資訊和經驗。
Acquire Vietnamese language possibility to exchange information and experience with accurate pronunciation and natural expressions in Vietnamese.
閱讀 Reading:
  * 能夠閱讀和理解越南報紙、雜誌和書籍的摘要資訊。
Be able to read and grasp the summary information which is drawn from Vietnamese newspapers, magazines, and books.
寫作 Writing:
  * 使用片語和混合句子書寫信件、文章和演講稿，如：對台灣外交，社群網站，都市變遷，旅遊業，現代社會等的想法。
  * Write letters, emails, articles, and presentations through the use of complex phrases and mixed sentences-for example: write comments and personal opinions about Taiwan Diplomacy, social networks, the urbanization process, tourism, modern society, …
  * 文化知識 Cultural knowledge:
  * 運用中高級程度的越語學習地區文化、鄉村文化、台越文化交流，等的文化知識。
  * Acquire cultural knowledge through the use of Vietnamese language at the upper-intermediate level with regards to cultural regions, village and community culture, Vietnam-Taiwan cultural exchange...
  * 越語檢定標準 Vietnamese language proficiency level:
  * 達到B2(中高級標準)
  * Be to reach B2(Upper-intermediate)
