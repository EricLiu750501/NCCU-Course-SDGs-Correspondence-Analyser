##  課程簡介Course Description
Intermediate Business Writing gives students an insight of the business world by exposing them to knowledge of the English language skills, especially in the writing skill area, necessary to succeed. Students will also receive academic writing training to develop the ability to produce well-organized, logical, and persuasive essays.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
This course aims to share tacit knowledge that allows students to develop their English written communication skills for use in a business context. It also shares with students tips to write academically to prepare themselves for more advanced academic work and international studies as exchange students.
