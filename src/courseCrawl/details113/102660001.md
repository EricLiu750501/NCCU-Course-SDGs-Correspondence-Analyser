##  課程簡介Course Description
Course description : This course aims to explore the current state and trends of education reforms across various countries. By comparing the education policies, systems, and practices of different nations, students will gain an understanding of the diversity and commonalities in education reforms. The course will cover case studies from multiple countries and include guest lectures or discussions with education experts from different regions, fostering a deep understanding of international education reforms.
課程簡介 : 本課程旨在探討全球各國教育改革的現狀與趨勢，通過比較不同國家的教育政策、制度和實踐，幫助學生了解教育改革的多樣性和共性。課程將涵蓋多個國家的案例研究，並邀請來自不同地區的教育專家進行講座或討論，促進學生對國際教育改革的深入理解。

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Course objectives and outcomes
  * Understand the background and motivations behind education reforms.
  * Grasp the relevant theories related to education reform.
  * Comprehend the education policies and practices of various countries.
  * Analyze the operations and case studies of education reforms.
  * Develop critical thinking and analytical skills through discussions.
  * Foster cross-cultural exchange and cooperation through discussions.
學習目標與成效
  * 瞭解教育改革的背景和動機。
  * 瞭解教育改革之相關理論。
  * 瞭解不同國家的教育政策和實踐。
  * 瞭解教育改革之運作與個案研究。
  * 培養批判性思維和分析能力並進行討論。
  * 促進跨文化交流和合作並進行討論。
