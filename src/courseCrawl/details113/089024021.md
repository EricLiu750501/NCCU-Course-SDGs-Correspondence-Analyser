##  課程簡介Course Description
1. 本課程適合具備高級華能力(TOCFL Band B2-C1/CEFR B1-B2/HSK6級)的外籍學生修習。
2. 本課程使學生能兼顧聽、說、讀、寫四方面的語言能力，並期望學生能透過課程進一步了解華語及臺灣文化。
This course is suitable for foreign students with an intermediate level of Chinese proficiency (TOCFL Band B2-C1/CEFR B1-B2/HSK6)
This course aims to enhance students' language skills in listening, speaking, reading, and writing, and expects students to gain a deeper understanding of Mandarin Chinese and Taiwanese culture through the course.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
課程內容涵蓋社會、科技、經濟、政治、文化、環境等多元議題探討，擴展學生對不同領域的語言運用。學生能針對不熟悉、抽象與複雜的主題進行假設與議論性的討論。學生也可整合不同資料做出適當結論，並且站在不同立場闡述不同觀點，發表一定篇幅的言論。  
The course content covers a wide range of topics, including society, technology, economics, politics, culture, and the environment, expanding students' language use across various fields. Students are able to hypothesize and engage in argumentative discussions on unfamiliar, abstract, and complex topics. They can also integrate different sources of information to draw appropriate conclusions and present different viewpoints from various perspectives, delivering statements of a certain length.

###### _出席相關規定_ _Attendance and Class Participation_
  1. 第一天上課必須出席。The first class attendance is required. 
  2. 每天上課必須在簽到單上簽名，遲到或是早退20分鐘以上，算缺席一個小時。
Students are required to sign the attendances sheet upon arrival of each class. Students who arrive 20 minutes later after the beginning of the class (or leave 20 minutes earlier) will be considered absent for one full hour.
  1. 因病請假需提供公立醫院診斷書 ，否則以缺席計。
A medical certificate from a PUBLIC HOSPITAL is required for leave due to illness, otherwise it will be counted as absence
  1. 缺席一小時扣總成績一分（最多扣十分），缺席超過40個小時，成績將以不及格計。
One point off for one hour of absence(up to 10). Students who are absent for more than 40 hours will be graded as failed in this class.
The regulations may be adjusted.
