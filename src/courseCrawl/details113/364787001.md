##  課程簡介Course Description
This course applies qualitative and quantitative analysis and decision-making tools to the practice of business diagnosis through experience learning. Students will develop relevant plans and tools through case studies and diagnosis of their own companies. Moreover, this course treats management consulting as a profession. It introduces the practices and philosophy of management consulting to students.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The goal is to transform the students from business executives to those who are able to solve managerial problems for others. To reach this goal, students will be asked to read papers about how to offer consulting services as well as comment on the reports prepared by some management consulting firms.
