##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is designed to help our students to understand how international business, international political economy, international security and international relations interact. As the transaction approach has become more and more common in trade, politics and diplomacy, it is necessary for our students to know more about how politics and economics interact together with the influence of technology, entrepreneurship, trade,organization, marketing and management on international relations as a whole. It is an interdisciplinary integrative approach to combine several subjects to meet the need of humanities and social science training in Chengda. Students will have opportunities to study more than the regular international relations and become more familiar with paradigm shifts that have happened in the age of AI and Metaverse.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The usual contents of history, historiography, philosophy, politics and economics will be included together with an introduction to international business, international political economy and international relations. It is a balancing class on theory, practice and case studies with an emphasis on the basic requirement of being "scientifically sound" and "socially relevant" as far as possible. Students can prepare themselves for further study in Chengda and elsewhere or pursue their careers afterward.
