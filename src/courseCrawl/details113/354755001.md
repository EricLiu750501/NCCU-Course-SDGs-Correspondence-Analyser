##  課程簡介Course Description
This course introduces the fundamental concepts of experimental design and statistical methods for analyzing experimental data. Topics covered include completely randomized designs, randomized block designs, Latin square designs, analysis of covariance, etc. The course will be conducted through lectures on theoretical foundations and methods, students will use computer programs to analyze data from designed experiments.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon completing this course, students should be able to:
  1. Understand the benefits and principles of experimental design.
  2. Properly conduct the process of practical experimental design.
  3. Comprehend the statistical models associated with various experimental designs.
  4. Use computer programs to analyze data from designed experiments appropriately and apply the results to decision-making.
