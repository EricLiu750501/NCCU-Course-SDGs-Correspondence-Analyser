##  課程簡介Course Description
Graph Theory is one of the oldest subareas of Discrete Mathematics. This course builds on the required undergraduate course on Discrete Mathematics (but is completely independent from it). The main purpose of the course is to get acquainted with the fundamentals of Graph Theory. The course requires no prerequisites except mathematical maturity and curiousity.
