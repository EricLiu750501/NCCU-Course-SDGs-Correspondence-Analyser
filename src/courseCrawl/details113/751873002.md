##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程的主題爲介紹微分方程。本學期主要專著在偏微分方程以及一些泛函分析（特別是弱導數以及分佈導數）。
The main theme of this course is to introduce differential equations. This semester will mainly focus on partial differential equations (PDE) and some functional analysis (especially weak derivatives and distributional derivatives).

我們預計根據下列計劃進行教學：
We will plan to deliver the ideas based on the followings:
  1. 先備知識以及介紹偏微分方程 Preliminaries and introduction to PDE 「第1週 1st week」
  2. 一階偏微分方程 First order PDE 「第2週 2nd week」
  3. 一維度波方程 1 dimensional wave equation 「第3週 3rd week」
  4. Duhamel 原則 Duhamel's principle 「第4週 4th week」
  5. n 維度波方程 n dimensional wave equation 「第5週 5th week」
  6. 弱導數以及分佈導數 weak derivatives and distributional derivatives 「第6週 6th week」
  7. Lax-Milgram 定理 Lax Milgram theorem 「第7週 7th week」
  8. 最大值原則 Maximum principle 「第8週 8th week」
  9. 期中報告 Midterm presentation 「第9週 9th week」
  10. 橢圓偏微分方程 Elliptic PDE 「第10週 10th week」
  11. 傅里葉級數 Fourier series 「第11-12週 11-12th week」
  12. 傅里葉變換 Fourier transform 「第13-14週 13-14th week」
  13. 卷積 convolution 「第15週 15th week」
  14. 期末報告 Final presentation 「第16週 16th week」
每週會有3節正課。建議每週花至少3小時自習。  
There will be 3 regular classes per week. It is recommend to spend at least 3 hours for self-study.
對於不熟悉（或不會使用） LaTeX 的學生，比起直接安裝 MiKTeX 或 TeX Live ，這里我建議可以從 Overleaf （線上編輯器，有許多模板以及使用說明）或 LyX （具有圖形界面輔助）開始，兩者皆免費。  
For those students who are not familiar to (or do not know how to use) LaTeX, rather than directly install MiLTeX or Tex Live, here I suggests you can start from Overleaf (online editor, including many templates and instructions) or LyX (which is a program with graphical user interface), both of them are free.
