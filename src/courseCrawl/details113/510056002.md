##  課程簡介Course Description
This course aims to introduce further grammatical topics and patterns beyond those learned in the first semester. The grammatical topics and patterns are grouped under different notions and functions for everyday life situations. Students will engage in various types of pattern drills to improve fluency and grammatical accuracy.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  * Students should be able to use correct grammatical items and patterns for everyday life situations.
  * Students should be able to understand differences among pattern choices for the same function or situation.
