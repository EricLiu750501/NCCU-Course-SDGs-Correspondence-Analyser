##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程主在帶領同學探討談判的理論、技巧，並經由實際案例，檢驗談判理論。課程的重點結合西方談判理論，以及東方的兵學藝術。所以孫子兵法的一些概念也會在課堂上介紹。
This course is attempted to explore the science and arts of bargaining and negotiation. Western negotiation theories will be introduced, investigated and blended with traditional Chinese strategic wisdom such as Sun Tzu’s Art of War. Active class participation and negotiation experience sharing is strongly encouraged.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
培養同學談判的分析能力與實戰能力。
To cultivate students’ abilities of negotiation and provide them with analytical framework for analyzing negotiation cases in real world.
After a 36-hour training in negotiation, students are expected to equip themselves with better
negotiation skills and deep analytical insight.
