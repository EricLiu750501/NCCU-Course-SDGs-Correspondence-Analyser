##  課程簡介Course Description
  1. Do you know what "real estate" is? You want to rent or buy a house? If you want to buy a house, you need to calculate the mortgage payment and your capability. For investment, you need to estimate the rate of return or appreciation. You can find the solution in this class. I'll also show you how to find the answers through computer function for AI quick approach!
  2. Hi, everyone! I'm professor Tsoyu Calvin LIN, from the Dept of Land Economics, National Chengchi University. I've been teaching and conducting research on real estate finance and investment for over 25 years. I received the Fulbright scholarship to UC Berkeley, EU's Erasmus + scholarship to UMCS in Poland, and Beijing University of Technology for lectures and research.
  3. After attending this class, you will have basic knowledge on real estate and its categories. You can analyze the financial feasibility of buying or renting a house, and investing on real estate. You'll also learn the technique in computer function and AI for solution.
  4. Besides the basic finance and investment foundation, I'll also show you some real cases as I served as the advisors for government and private sectors for past 25 years. I'll also introduce a quick method for investment valuation in computer functions, which you can not find in other courses.
  5. In addition to students from land economics or real estate management, anyone interested in real estate industry are all welcome to join this class.
  6. You'll have clear understanding on buying houses or real estate investment. You will not make wrong decision merely according to other people's suggestions or commercials from media.
  7. Don't hesitate! Join us! You might be the next real estate Tycoon, or the successor of Donald Trump or Warren Buffet!

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. "知道什麼是""不動產""嗎？要租房子或買房子呢？買房子要精算自己的償還能力，投資也要算投報率或推算增值空間，這些問題都可在本課程得到解答，老師還要教你怎麼用電腦程式AI人工智慧快速求解哦！"
  2. "大家好，我是政治大學地政系林左裕教授，教授與研究不動產投資與財務超過25年，曾接受Fulbright獎學金赴美國加州柏克萊大學、歐盟Erasmus+獎學金赴波蘭居禮夫人大學、以及赴中國北京科技大學為講座教授及研究。我的著作""不動產投資管理""自2000年出版至今，是目前世界上最受歡迎的中文不動產教科書哦!"
  3. "上完課後，你將對不動產及其類型有初步的認識，未來在住宅租或買的選擇上，會更有財務能力的考量；在投資的決策上，也會有投報率的決策依據。這些過程，都會有科學化及自動化的電腦程式輔助你完成。"
  4. 除了財務及投資分析的基礎外，老師要把這二十幾年在政府及業界擔任顧問所經歷過的實例經驗帶給大家，並應用簡易的電腦功能及網路平台，教大家迅速求解的方法，這是在其他課程學不到的！
  5. 除了地政系、土地管理系的學生外，目前及未來想從事不動產相關領域的專業人士，都歡迎加入本課程。
  6. 你將會對購屋及未來投資決策有更清楚的認識，避免因道聽塗說或媒體渲染而做出錯誤的決策。
  7. "不要猶豫！加入本課程！你可能就是下一個""房產大富翁""，甚至是川普或巴菲特的接班人喔！"
