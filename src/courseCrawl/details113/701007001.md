##  課程簡介Course Description
The purpose of this course is to offer an introduction to mathematical statistics, emphasizing the theory and methods used to analyze data. Key topics include a brief review of probability theory, estimation, hypothesis testing, and fundamental inferential techniques. The course will focus on building a solid understanding of the theoretical foundations of statistics, while also incorporating applied and theoretical examples. Students will gain skills in modeling uncertainty and using mathematical tools to analyze data.
Prerequisites : Probability Thoery, Calculus and basic Linear Algebra

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon the completion of this course, students will be able to:
  1. Understand the fundamental principles of probability theory and how they relate to statistical analysis.
  2. Work with different types of random variables and probability distributions, including discrete and continuous types.
  3. Apply the concepts of expected value, variance, and higher moments to random variables.
  4. Understand and apply methods of statistical estimation, including point estimation and interval estimation.
  5. Perform hypothesis testing and interpret the results of hypothesis tests.
  6. Apply key statistical theorems (such as the Central Limit Theorem) to solve real-world problems.
