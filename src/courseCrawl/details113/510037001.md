##  課程簡介Course Description
This course is designed to provide the economic overview for Southeast Asia in term of the development and integration processes, prospects and challenges. We explore the variations in regional economic growth of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) member within 10 countries (Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, Indonesia, Brunei, Cambodia, Laos and Myanmar). This course is aimed to bring students into deeper approach about ASEAN economies. A map will be constructed throughout the course with selective economic conditions for comparison and better understanding.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Students develop their critical responses through the insightful knowledge, rigorous analysis, and the sharing of best practices. By the end of the course, students will be able to obtain the following knowledge:
  * Evolution of ASEAN cooperation and institutional structure.
  * The role of government and foreign envestment in ASEAN economic growth and development.
  * The causes of the 1997 Asian crisis and its impacts on the ASEAN economies.
  * The Covid-19 pandamic social-economic shocks; its impact and response on the ASEAN economies.
  * The environmental consequences of industrialization and growth.
  * Trade patterns, transitional economies and the implications of China’s economic growth to ASEAN countries.
  * Prospects and challenges for ASEAN economic cooperation. 
  * Macroeconomic assessment and economic outlook from short-term to long-term.
