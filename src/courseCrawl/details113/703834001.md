##  課程簡介Course Description
Natural language processing (NLP) is an attractive area in artifical intelligence. A wide range of technologies were investigated for enabling the machine to communicate with humans. With the progress of machine learning, the power of NLP has been shown in novel applications in the areas such as fintech, medical, and digital humanity. 
This course will introduce the fundamental topics of NLP from lexicon, syntax, to pragmatics and show how to formulate the language tasks with machine learning. The most recent, cutting-edge approaches to NLP such as Transformer-based large language models (LLMs) are the focus of this course. Real world data will be used in the final project. The participants are expected to handle the textual data for solving real world problems.
Special Note: During the semester, the instructor may occasionally attend important meetings, which could result in temporary adjustments to the teaching format for that week (e.g., switching to asynchronous online teaching).
自然語言處理包含一系列技術，讓電腦具備語言理解與生成的能力。搭配深度學習等技術，如今自然語言處理在許多人工智慧應用中展現了強大的威力，並且進一步朝向更具有前膽性的議題發展，應用領域包含金融科技、醫學、數位人文等。
本課程擬從詞彙、句法、語用等不同層次介紹自然語言處理的技術進展，示範如何用機率與統計來解決語言的問題，以及最前沿的機器學習模型例如基於 Transformer 的大型語言模型（LLM），在自然言處理的應用。並且提供真實資料作為專題素材，從中練習掌握自然語言處理的技巧與實務。
特別注意：授課教師學期中將不定時出席重要會議，因此可能會發生臨時調整當週授課模式（例如改為非同步線上授課）的況狀。

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. Understand the fundamental topics from lexicon, syntax, to pragmatics in natural language processing and computational linguistics.
  2. Be familiar with basic NLP like segmentation, part-of-speech tagging, syntatic parsing, and dependency parsing.
  3. Learn to build machine learning models with linguistic features for NLP from scratch. 
  4. Be aware of the most recent progress in large language models (LLMs) and apply LLMs in real applications.
  1. 了解詞彙、句法、語用等不同層面的計算語言學議題
  2. 學習斷詞、詞性標記、句法剖析、相依性分析的原理與應用情境
  3. 熟悉如何在不使用現成函式庫的情況下，自行撰寫 Python 程式打造機器學習模型，搭配適當的語言特徵來分析文件
  4. 認識大型語言模型的最新進展，並善用於應用中。
