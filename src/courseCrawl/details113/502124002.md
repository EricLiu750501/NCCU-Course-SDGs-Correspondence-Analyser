##  課程簡介Course Description
This course offers an advanced level of Arabic for Arabic majors in the fourth year. Students are required to study and read closely longer complex texts on different subjects, including primary sources. Students will learn how to synthesise and re-structure the reading material in an engaging and analytical manner. In addition, students will practise conversational skills and engage in topical discussion.
