##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程課程旨於引領修課同學從歷史、族群、文學三大基礎面向，探討分析捷克民族如何發展成當今的社會面貌，包括歷史發展與國族意識、語言族群與認同、作為語言現象的文學媒介等，進而認識捷克社會的形成與建構。同時，本課程根據上述面向邀請國際協同師資，規劃共七次線上主題講座以及一次線上工作坊，授課對象設定為全校學生，無捷克語文先修課程要求，並以英文為主要授課語言。此外，本校開課教師除了全程參與講座之外，也會在學期分階段輔以中文統整課程主題內容，幫助修課學生循序漸進地理解整個課程的核心概念。
This course is designed to guide students in exploring and analyzing the development of the Czech nation into its current social appearance, focusing on three aspects: history, ethnicity, and literature. Topics include the historical development and national consciousness, language groups and identity, and literature as a linguistic phenomenon. The course also emphasizes understanding the formation and construction of Czech society. To enhance learning, Czech collaborative lecturers will be invited, and the course will consist of seven online lectures and one online workshop. It is open to all students, with no requirement for prior knowledge of the Czech language, and English will be the primary language of instruction. Additionally, during the course, explanations in Chinese will be provided from time to time to help students understand the course content more clearly.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程主要教學目標為系統性介紹捷克社會的形成與建構，引導學生認識當前捷克社會，並增進對中東歐地區的瞭解，同時也藉由國際協同師資豐富課程主題內容，營造在地國際化教學環境，培育學生具世界觀的觀察力與思辨力，進而造就學生國際人文素養。
The primary objectives of this course are to provide a systematic introduction to the formation and development of Czech society, help students achieve a deeper understanding of contemporary Czech society, and enhance their knowledge regarding Central and Eastern Europe. Additionally, the course enriches its content through collaboration with Czech scholars. This approach aims to cultivate students’ observation and critical thinking skills from a global perspective.
