##  課程簡介Course Description
學生課前詳細閱讀個案教材，進行深度之分組討論，上課時參與互動教學，藉此過程協助學生理論與實務之整合。
Integrate theory and practices through series of case studies and discussion.

能力項目說明
  * A. 習得相關進階專業知識（Students will acquire and apply expertise in their respective fields.）
  * B. 理解與辨別商業環境中道德、社會責任與永續議題（Students will understand and be able to identify the ethics, social responsibility, and sustainability considerations of business situations.）
  * C. 創新方法、創業態度與面對變革的能力（Students can demonstrate innovative, entrepreneurial approaches to manage change.）

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
經由個案研討，結合理論與實務，並藉此機回顧所學理論之實用價值，並提升學生之聽說讀想之能力。
Integrate theory and practices through series of case studies and discussion,in order to enhance students’various management skills.
