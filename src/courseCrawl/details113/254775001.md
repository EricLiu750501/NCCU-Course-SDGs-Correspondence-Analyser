##  課程簡介Course Description
本課程提供對社會網絡的實證和理論研究的廣泛介紹。我們將涵蓋經典和當代研究，從基本定義開始，然後涉及一系列主題，包括網絡形成和結構的模型（同質性、焦點、社群）；網絡上的動態過程（傳染、影響和疾病模型）；合作網絡；個人網絡；線上網絡；以及網絡抽樣和數據收集。本課程的內容旨在吸引來自多個學科背景的學生，包括社會學、心理學和相關領域的學生。
This course provides a broad introduction to the empirical and theoretical study of social networks. We will cover classic and contemporary studies, beginning with fundamental definitions, and then moving through a range of topics, including models of network formation and structure (homophily, foci, communities); dynamic processes on networks (contagion, influence, and disease models); collaborative networks; personal networks; online networks; and network sampling and data collection. The course material is intended to be of interest to students from a wide range of disciplinary backgrounds, including sociology, psychology, and related fields.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程有三個目標。首先，透過閱讀論文和討論，本課程將使學生熟悉一些關於社會網絡的經典和當代研究。其次，本課程將促進學生對分析個人網絡的基本概念和原則的理解。最後，本課程將提升學生的英文閱讀和課堂報告的能力。
This course has three goals.
1. First, this course will familiarize students some classic and contemporary research about social networks through reading papers and discussing them
2. Second, this course will promote students’ understanding of the basic concepts and principles in the analysis of personal networks
3. Finally, this course will improve students’ reading and presentation skills.
