##  課程簡介Course Description
This year-long course builds upon students' existing academic writing and reading skills, further enhancing their proficiency. In the second semester, the writing component focuses on logical reasoning and the development of research paper writing skills. Students will write a comparison/contrast essay and a research paper. The reading component emphasizes the development of critical thinking and analytical skills, applied both to other authors' works and to their own writing. Through in-class exercises and writing workshops, students will refine their written English, cultivating a more effective and polished writing style. Additionally, they will grow as critical readers, learning to distinguish between different writing styles and provide constructive feedback. Ultimately, the course equips students with the ability to revise and edit their work effectively through self-reflection, while fostering an appreciation for the choices made throughout the writing process.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Upon semester completion, students will be able to: 
  1. Navigate all stages of the writing process with proficiency. 
  1. Establish a solid foundation in writing a well-structured comparison/contrast essay and an original research paper 
  1. Discern effective writing styles from less effective ones and apply this knowledge to both self and peer review for error correction. 
  1. Utilize critical thinking to analyze peer writing and offer constructive feedback. 
  1. Engage effectively with diverse research sources, enhancing their writing through reading, critical evaluation, and participation in class discussions.
