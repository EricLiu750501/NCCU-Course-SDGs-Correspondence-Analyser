##  課程簡介Course Description
經貿越語針對已達中級越語能力的學生，提升其能力至高級程度。在語言與經貿兩方面的平衡下，本課程主要幫助學生對越南有更高的瞭解與掌握度，以協助他們未來到越南或從事與越南相關領域的商業職涯做好準備。運用以學生為中心的學習技巧，課程的大部分時間將會以課前的小組準備為基礎來進行討論與活動。學生預期能夠透過討論以增強越語的口說流暢度，以及提升思辨能力。
This course is designed for students who have already achieved an intermediate competence in Vietnamese so as to develop their ability at the advanced level. With a balance between linguistic, economy and trade content, this course is aimed to help students gain a better understanding of Vietnam, which will help prepare them for business careers in Vietnam or in Vietnam-related environments. Using Student-Centered Learning Strategies, most of the class time of this course will be spent in discussions and activities based on group preparation before class time. Students are expected to enhance fluency in spoken Vietnamese and develop critical thinking skills through discussion.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
本課程著重於透過專業口語的衍生詞彙與文法，為學生建立國際貿易能力的穩固基礎。學生能夠參與各式主題會話。到學期末，學生將得以增進以下能力：
This course aims to reinforce a solid foundation in international trade skills by providing extended vocabulary and grammar of the professional spoken language. Students will be able to participate in engaging conversations on a wide range of topics. By the end of the semester, students will obtain as follow:
發音 Pronunciation:
· 運用越南語自然聲調習得標準發音。
Obtain perfect pronunciation with natural intonation in the Vietnamese language.
詞彙 Vocabulary:
· 掌握與經貿越語、越南風俗及傳統等相關之特定詞彙。
Obtain specific vocabulary with regard to Vietnamese for economy and trade, Vietnamese customs, and traditions.
· 能適當運用同義詞和反義詞。
Be able to use effectively synonyms and antonyms.
文法 Grammar:
· 習得進階混合句、片語的用法，如thực tế...cho thấy…; theo kết quả nghiên cứu …; không những không... mà ngược lại còn…; là..., không kể với...hay ...; xét về bản chất, …; lý do để... là vì... ; tuy nhiên …nên... ; không chỉ là ... mà cũng…; …
Obtain the addition of mixed sentences, the usage of complex phrases for example- thực tế...cho thấy…; theo kết quả nghiên cứu …; không những không... mà ngược lại còn…; là..., không kể với...hay ...; xét về bản chất, …; lý do để... là vì... ; tuy nhiên …nên...; không chỉ là ... mà cũng…; …
聽力 Listening:
· 能夠聆聽商務貿易會話，並在廣泛的詞彙和文法範圍內，瞭解各種情況下之不同表達方式的差異。
Be able to listen to business and trade conversations; understand differences of diverse expressions in a variety of situations within the wide range of vocabulary and grammar.
口說 Speaking:
· 能夠熟練地使用越南語，並在不同的商務場合以自然的語調進行有效的交流。
Be able to acquire speaking proficiency to effectively communicate in different business situations with natural intonation in Vietnamese.
閱讀 Reading:
· 閱讀和辨識各式主題中較長的口語文字涵義。
Read and identify the purpose of longer spoken texts with complexity in various topics.
寫作 Writing:
· 以制定商務會議、向客戶發表簡報、商務協商、投訴、糾紛解決等特殊商務情境主題進行有效文件之寫作。
Write effective documents in particular business situations such as: making a business appointment; doing a presentation to customers; a business negotiation; resolving complaints and disputes, …
文化知識 Cultural knowledge:
· 透過在各種情況下的越南語使用以學習商業文化知識。
Acquire business cultural knowledge through the use of the Vietnamese language in different situations.
越語檢定標準 Vietnamese language proficiency level:
· 優異的學生將能夠達到越語C1程度
Successful students will be able to reach C1
