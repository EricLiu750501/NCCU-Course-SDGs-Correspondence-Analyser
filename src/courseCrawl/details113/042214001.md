##  課程簡介Course Description
This course embarks on a comprehensive exploration of resilience, tracing its manifestations through literature, psychology, medical science, ecology, urban planning, economics, and more. Over 18 weeks, students will engage with classic texts, expert insights, and hands-on activities and a parade that highlight resilience's many forms—from biological adaptation to societal endurance.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
  1. Understand the Concept of Resilience in Multiple Contexts and Scales
  2. Critically Analyze Resilience in Classic and Contemporary Texts
  3. Connect Theory to Real-World Challenges
  4. Develop Psychological and Emotional Resilience Skills
  5. Collaborate and Communicate Effectively Across Disciplines
  6. Reflect on Personal and Collective Resilience Across Cultures
  7. Apply Resilience Strategies in Creative and Practical Ways
