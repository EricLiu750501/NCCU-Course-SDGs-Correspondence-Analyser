##  課程簡介Course Description
The intended students are those who are more advanced and are ready to write their theses. The class will be conducted on a face-to-face basis. We will cover topics related to an individual student’s thesis, and discuss relevant papers. The student is required to write a publishable paper in the end of the semester.
