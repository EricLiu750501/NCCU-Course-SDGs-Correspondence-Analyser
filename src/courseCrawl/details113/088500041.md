##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Topic |  Function |  Grammar |  Culture  
---|---|---|---  
Pronunciation  |  Introduce initials, vowels, pronunciation, positions, tones and spelling, as well as introduce five sets of basic conversations. |  -Everyday Language -Classroom phrases |  Simple replies in everyday life.  
What is your name? |  Introduce the ”last name + title” form of address, as well as the polite way to ask for someone’s last name. |  -Sentences with verbs姓、叫、是 -Simple type of questions with the particle嗎 -Questions with a question word -abbreviated questions with the particle呢 |  Responses used the first-time meeting someone.  
Hello, good morning |  Practice the most popular greetings and titles. |  -Simple sentences with stative verbs -Stative verb-not-stative verb question |  Polite ways to greet and introduce one another.  
I like to watch movies |  Introduce the basic uses of the verbs “看、要、買、喜歡” and simple conversations for students at the store or in a restaurant. |  -S.-V.-O. sentences -V-not-V questions -Sentences with the Auxiliary verbs -Transposed objects |  Discussing one another’s interests.  
How much is the pen? |  Introduce the numbers 0 to 99 and practice asking for the prices of things. |  -Quantified nouns -Sums of money -Specified and numbered nouns -Price per unit -Sentences with direct object and indirect object |  Communication between buyer and seller.
