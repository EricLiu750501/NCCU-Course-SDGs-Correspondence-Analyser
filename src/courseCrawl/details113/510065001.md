##  課程簡介Course Description
In-depth knowledge of Thai language and culture including values, beliefs and attitudes in contemporary Thai society. Development of upper intermediate Thai language skills by studying contemporary Thai literature, understanding of the complexity of Thai culture from various topics including cultural values and attitudes in politics, economics, religion, and international and cross-cultural relations, tracing these back to their social and historic roots.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
Reading comprehension in texts from contemporary Thai literature with a more specialised vocabulary. Knowledge and in-depth understanding of the complexity of Thai language and culture. Understanding differences between spoken and written language. Ability in academic discussion. Upper intermediate Thai language proficiency and ability to give oral presentation and to write essays in an academic context.
