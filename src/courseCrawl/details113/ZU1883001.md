##  課程簡介Course Description
The aim of this course is to teach the application of generative AI technology for solving real-world problems in collaboration with three companies. Throughout the course, these companies will assign representatives to the classroom who will introduce their business models, operational challenges, expectations for generative AI, and actively participate in weekly course activities. The results of this course will not only be showcased during the ICI Joint-presentation but also submitted to the participating enterprises if the quality of the work is recognized.
