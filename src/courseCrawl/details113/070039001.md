##  課程簡介Course Description
課程會對華語詞彙進行全面性的介紹。在本課程中，我們會針對以下幾個華語詞彙呈現的特性進行深入介紹與探討: (a) 華語中關於字、詞、詞組的概念；(b) 華語中關於字、詞、詞組的界線；(c) 華語詞彙的建構以及其內部結構；(d) 漢字與華語詞彙的不對稱性；(e) 華語詞彙與華語語法的互動；(f) 華語詞彙的功能性; (g) 華語詞彙與詞意等面向。本課程除了幫助學生建立華語詞彙的基本觀念之外，也會透過課堂討論來引導學生觀察日常生活中的詞彙現象進行描述，並適時結合一些當代華語的相關語言研究內容，幫助學生掌握華語詞彙的研究脈絡；本課程同時也會不時以中英對比的方式(如詞彙形式、語法差異)進行不同語言之間的比較與說明，幫助學生掌握華語詞彙與華語語法的互動以及培養學生具有能力洞察不同語言之間的差異，以期未來能反饋到華語教學實務上。
This course provides an introduction to Chinese morphology. In particular, we look at many different aspects in which the features of Chinese morphology manifest: (a) the concept of Chinese words; (b) the internal structure and boundary of Chinese words; (c) the formation of Chinese words; (d) the relation between Chinese characters and Chinese words; (e) the interaction between Chinese words and Chinese grammar; (f) the function of Chinese words; (g) the lexical meaning of Chinese words; (h) loan words and the development of new words in Chinese; (i) the relation between Chinese words and culture. Overall, this course offers a foundation (regarding the knowledge of Chinese words) for students who aspire to become a Chinese-as-a-second-language teacher so that they can apply the learned knowledge to their teaching of Chinese in the future.

Course Objectives
This course provides an introduction to Chinese morphology. In particular, we look at many different aspects in which the features of Chinese morphology manifest: (a) the concept of Chinese words; (b) the internal structure and boundary of Chinese words; (c) the formation of Chinese words; (d) the relation between Chinese characters and Chinese words; (e) the interaction between Chinese words and Chinese grammar; (f) the function of Chinese words; (g) the lexical meaning of Chinese words; (h) loan words and the development of new words in Chinese; (i) the relation between Chinese words and culture. Overall, this course offers a foundation (regarding the knowledge of Chinese words) for students who aspire to become a Chinese-as-a-second-language teacher so that they can apply the learned knowledge to their teaching of Chinese in the future.
Learning Outcomes
1. To better understand (a) the key features of Chinese morphology; and (b) the interaction between Chinese words and other components such as meaning, grammar and Chinese culture
2. To develop the teaching and communication skills of applying the learned knowledge about Chinese words to teaching of Chinese. 
3. To develop the analytical skills of recognizing the morphological differences between Chinese and other languages, and elaborating on how Chinese words interact with meaning, grammar and Chinese culture.
4. To develop critical thinking skills.
