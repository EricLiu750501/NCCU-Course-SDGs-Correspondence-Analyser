##  課程簡介Course Description
The goal of this course is to provide an introduction to human factors research and examine human-automation design with special emphasis on user experience (UX). Each class will introduce some UX-related issues and explore theories and applications relevant to solving the problems. Topics focus on how to analyze, create and improve the design of intelligent and mobile systems to enable these applications to be compatible with human capabilities and expectations.

##  課程目標與學習成效Course Objectives & Learning Outcomes 
The purpose of this course is to provide the basic knowledge of UX. Upon successful completion, students will:
  * Build a connection between UX and usability evaluation
  * Develop strong knowledge and skills on human interaction with intelligent applications and mobile systems
  * Gain experience in the design and evaluation of human-automation systems

  1. Course Overview: what is UX and why it matters
